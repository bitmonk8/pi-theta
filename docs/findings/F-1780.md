## Finding

The `tools:` field description in `docs/spec_topics/frontmatter/frontmatter-fields-b-and-templates.md` (line 28, the last bullet of the **Resolution snapshot** Consequences list) reads in full:

> - Per-query overrides and a project → loom → query cascade are deferred (see [Future Considerations](../future-considerations.md)).

The bullet is purely a deferral pointer — it states no loom 1.0 normative obligation, no shape of the deferred feature, and no carrier the deferral rides on. The same item is already inventoried as a first-class entry on the canonical surface-extensions page (`docs/spec_topics/future-considerations/surface-extensions.md` line 24, "**Per-query overrides for `model`, `tools`, and `system`** (project → loom → query cascade)"), with the loom 1.0 seam anchored at `docs/spec_topics/query/query-failure-and-repair.md` line 5 (the "loom 1.0 seam — per-call timeout / per-query overrides" callout on the runtime-internal options record). The frontmatter bullet duplicates that inventory entry without naming the seam, so a reader who follows the link arrives at the future-considerations hub and must search again for the actual entry.

Worse, the bullet lives at the *Consequences-of-the-resolution-snapshot* indentation level, alongside the four bullets that pin loom 1.0 behaviour for Pi-tool unregistration, file-watcher reload, `.loom`-callee reload, and Pi-extension hot-reload. Those four are consequences of the frozen-table contract this section establishes; the deferral bullet is unrelated metadata about a feature that doesn't exist in loom 1.0, slipped into the list under the same `-` prefix and with no separator. An implementer auditing the section's loom 1.0 obligations will not be misled in behaviour, but the bullet pollutes the normative contract and the surface-extensions page silently grows out-of-sync with this fork (e.g. if the inventory's wording changes or its seam anchor moves, the bullet here will not be updated and starts to drift).

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Delete the bullet outright. The Consequences list under **Resolution snapshot** would terminate at the existing Pi-extension hot-reload bullet (currently the penultimate entry).

Rationale carried by the deletion: the canonical inventory of deferred surface extensions is owned by `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`, and the loom 1.0 seam that lets the deferral land additively is owned by `query/query-failure-and-repair.md`'s "Options surface" callout. Neither owner needs the frontmatter shard to forward-cite it — no normative obligation on the `tools:` field rides on the deferral, and a reader who needs the deferred surface will find it on the index it's homed in. A bare cross-reference here would still misplace metadata about a non-loom-1.0 feature inside a Consequences-of-the-frozen-table list, so the cleanest fix is removal rather than re-skinning.

Implementer edge: confirm no other shard cross-references this bullet by anchor or back-link before deleting (the bullet has no anchor; a corpus-wide `grep -F 'project → loom → query cascade'` should match only the two owners above plus this bullet's old location, both of which survive the edit).

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

The bullet pollutes a normative loom 1.0 contract list with a deferral pointer that duplicates the canonical inventory entry on the surface-extensions page, and slowly drifts as the canonical entry evolves. No loom 1.0 behaviour is affected; no implementer is misled.
