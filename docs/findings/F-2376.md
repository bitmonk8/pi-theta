## Problem

`V9b` bundles three functionally distinct concerns in one leaf: registration steps 1–5, the `ReloadFailureInjector` watcher-time reload-failure-injection seam, and the model-reference-matcher production wiring (the load-pass construction of loom's exact-match resolver over `ctx.modelRegistry.getAvailable()` and its injection into the `V6a` parser seam). The matcher concern reads a different contract (`host-interfaces-core.md#model-registry-pin` and the matcher semantics `V11a` owns), carries its own Tests group, is the sole reason `V9b` lists `V6a` in `Deps`, and is witnessed by `V11a`/`V11a-T` via their cross-resolution reconciliation. Bundling it forces a reviewer to trace three independent contracts in one leaf, and a red-green failure in any concern blocks the others from landing even though the matcher-construction property is independently testable.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit-introduction
**Introducing commits:** 5953b18
**History:** The model-reference-matcher production-wiring point was assigned to `V9b` in `5953b18` ("resolve V6a model-reference-matcher seam has no declared producer", 2026-06-11), which added the matcher Adds sentence and the `V6a` `Deps` edge to a leaf that already carried registration steps 1–5 and the watcher-time reload failure-injection seam. The matcher was later re-specified as loom's own exact-match resolver in `2424003` (F-1311, 2026-06-24) and the originally co-resident drain-state contract was carved out to `V9m`, but the matcher concern was never separated, leaving the three-concern composition this finding flags. Prior bundling fixes (`c1fa860` on V9b obligation sub-IDs, `cb8737a` on corpus-wide over-bundling) did not touch the matcher.

## Solution approach

Split the model-reference-matcher production wiring out of `V9b` into a new sibling leaf that owns matcher construction and injection — move the matcher Adds sentence, the matcher Tests bullet, and the matcher clause of `V9b`'s Ships-when, mirroring the Tests move into `V9b-T`. Give the new leaf `Deps` on `V6a` and `V9b`, and drop `V6a` from `V9b`'s `Deps` since the matcher injection was its only justification. Retarget `V11a`/`V11a-T` so the cross-resolution reconciliation bullet, the `Deps`, and `V11a`'s Ships-when name the new matcher leaf instead of `V9b`, and link the new leaf into `docs/plan.md`'s V9 list.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the `ReloadFailureInjector` reload-failure-injection seam stays in `V9b` with the registry-swap and `.loom`/`.warp` re-parse arms — only the matcher concern moves (the seam is owned alongside F-2327's cluster).
- Do not add `V11a` to the new leaf's `Deps`: `V11a` depends on the matcher leaf, so a reverse edge would close a dependency cycle.
- Allocate the new leaf's ID per the plan's `V<N><letter>` scheme and no-invented-IDs policy (next free letter in the target phase; never reuse a retired ID), with its `-T` tests pair per `conventions.md`.
