## Finding

`V4c`'s `ERR-12` **Tests** bullet carries a scope-deferral note in addition to its acceptance criterion. The bullet states the harness-modelled assertion ("ERR-8 holds inside a subagent loom, exercised via the `H4a` harness modelling a subagent-mode callee") and then continues with a note that the delegated live-surface re-assertion "is a **real-host-only behaviour** — not asserted under `npm test`, it is witnessed at the manual real-host smoke gate ([`real-host-smoke-gate.md`] criterion (c)), the live-surface re-assertion `V9i` carries off the `H4a` double."

Per `conventions.md` §Leaf format, a **Tests** bullet is one assertion per REQ-ID — what the leaf claims to verify — and the leaf's gate is its **Ships when** (`npm test` proves `ERR-8`…`ERR-12`). Mixing a "not asserted under `npm test`, witnessed elsewhere" deferral into the bullet puts non-`npm-test` scope content inside the normative acceptance criterion: the bullet asserts a thing the **Ships when** gate claims `npm test` proves, while the same bullet carves part of it out as out-of-`npm-test`. The deferral is real and belongs in the leaf, but its home is descriptive scope prose (`Adds.`), not the Tests assertion.

The paired tests leaf `V4c-T` keeps the clean form of this bullet ("…modelling a subagent-mode callee — not the live `V9i` surface"), so the embedded note is an asymmetry between the implementation leaf and its `-T` partner as well as a §Leaf-format placement defect.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** introduced-by-commit (multi-commit interaction)
**Introducing commits:** `7bd65a7` (`pi-loom plan: resolve "V4c/V4f defer ERR-8 to ERR-13 live-surface confirmation to downstream owners that never re-assert it"`, 2026-06-18) introduced the cross-leaf scope clause into the `ERR-12` Tests bullet; `c768005` (`pi-loom spec-plan: fix F-1363 — relocate V9i ERR-8 real-session witness to smoke gate`, 2026-06-25) reshaped that clause into the current "real-host-only behaviour — not asserted under `npm test` … witnessed at the smoke gate" deferral note.
**History:** Before `7bd65a7` the `ERR-12` bullet was the clean form still present in `V4c-T` ("…modelling a subagent-mode callee — not the live `V9i` surface"). `7bd65a7` appended a delegated-live-carrier clause to the `V4c` (implementation) bullet only; `c768005`, fixing an unrelated relocation finding, rewrote that clause into the explicit `npm test` deferral note this finding flags. The defect therefore did not exist at inception; it accreted across these two later edits while `V4c-T` was left in its original clean shape.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Restore the `V4c` `ERR-12` **Tests** bullet to a single harness-modelled assertion and relocate the real-host deferral to `Adds.` as bounded-scope context.

- In `docs/plan_topics/V4c-terminal-outcomes.md`, reduce the `ERR-12` Tests bullet to the harness-modelled acceptance criterion only, e.g.: `` `ERR-12`: ERR-8 holds inside a subagent loom, exercised via the `H4a` harness modelling a subagent-mode callee. `` (matching the clean form already in `V4c-T`).
- Move the deferral content into the leaf's `Adds.` sentence as a scope annotation, e.g.: "the delegated ERR-8 live-surface re-assertion on the real subagent `AgentSession` is real-host-only — owned by `V9i` and witnessed at the manual real-host smoke gate ([`real-host-smoke-gate.md`](./real-host-smoke-gate.md) criterion (c)), out of scope for this leaf's `npm test` gate."

When relocating, reword the "the live-surface re-assertion `V9i` carries off the `H4a` double" phrase to an unambiguous form (e.g. "owned by `V9i`, which exercises it through the real host rather than the `H4a` session double used here") — see the related `"carries off the H4a double"` finding, which the same edit resolves. `V4c-T`'s `ERR-12` bullet already carries the clean form and needs no change. The smoke-gate cross-reference and the `V9i` ownership statement are preserved (do not delete the deferral — only move it out of the Tests assertion).

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

The leaf's **Ships when** states `npm test` proves `ERR-8`…`ERR-12`, while the `ERR-12` Tests bullet itself carves out a real-host-only sub-assertion as not-under-`npm-test`; a reader reconciling the two cannot tell from the Tests section alone what the leaf must assert to pass its own gate. The harness-modelled assertion is stated clearly enough that an implementer still produces a working leaf, so this degrades clarity rather than blocking pickability.
