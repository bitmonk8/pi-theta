## Problem

Both `V9l` and `V9l-T` carry a multi-clause blocking notice inside their **Deps** field, appended after the bare leaf-ID list with an em-dash. `conventions.md` §Leaf format defines **Deps.** as "Other leaf IDs that must be complete first" and ties it to the tag-based pickability predicate — it is not a home for open-question / risk prose. The blocking status is already recorded canonically in `plan.md` §Blocked obligations and annotated on the V9l link in the V9 slice list, and the block is enforced structurally by the real `V18c` dependency edge, so the Deps prose is explanatory duplication in a field reserved for build-order leaf IDs.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commit:** `55ed974` (2026-06-12) — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"degraded-state obligations green over open spec contradiction\""
**History:** Both `V9l-session-only-degraded-branch.md` and `V9l-T-session-only-degraded-branch.md` were created in `55ed974` (confirmed as the new-file commit via `git log --diff-filter=A`), and that inception commit already carried the blocking-status prose inside the Deps field of each (`git log -G "blocked.*until the version-bump"` first reports it at `55ed974`, which `git show 55ed974:…V9l…` confirms verbatim). The later commit `9c42f2d` (2026-06-16) relocated the fallback path into `plan.md` §Blocked obligations but left the Deps-field prose in both leaves unchanged, so the placement defect has stood since the leaf pair's inception.

## Solution approach

Trim the **Deps** field of both `V9l` and `V9l-T` to the bare leaf-ID list required by `conventions.md` §Leaf format, removing the embedded blocking-status prose. Relocate that explanatory prose — that `V18c` owns the clause-(a) resolution and the leaf is not pickable while the host-prerequisites clause (a) contradiction stands — into `plan.md` §Blocked obligations, which already carries the canonical V9l blocked entry. A terse pointer to `plan.md` §Blocked obligations may remain at the leaf.

## Solution constraints

- Keep the fixed `conventions.md` §Leaf-format field set; do not introduce a new per-leaf field (e.g. a Status/Blocked section) to hold the relocated prose.
- Retain the `V18c` Deps edge in both leaves — it is what structurally enforces the block.
