## Finding

The V9-slice **Interleave note** in `docs/plan.md` (┬¦"V9 ŌĆö Extension host integration") exists to flag the cross-slice edges that make V9 non-contiguous: it documents that `V11a` lands mid-V9 (`V9b ŌåÆ V11a ŌåÆ V9c`/`V9i`/`V9j`), that `V9h`/`V9g`/`V9l` reach forward to `V18c`, and closes with "Sequence by **Deps**, not slice number."
