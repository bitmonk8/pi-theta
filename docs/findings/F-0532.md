## Finding

`V9g`'s **Adds** field declares the `session_shutdown` teardown handler emits "the bare / two-token / three-token fallback wire forms" for its `loom/host/*` and `loom/runtime/*` `console.error` diagnostics. These are normative MUSTs in [`diagnostic-emission-isolation.md`](../spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/diagnostic-emission-isolation.md): on a **serialiser throw** the catch arm MUST emit `console.error(code)` (bare) for the three flat-`details` codes, and the two-token form `` `${code} ${detailsEventReason}` `` for the two nested-shape codes (`loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded`, `loom/runtime/cancelled-by-session-shutdown`); on a **construction-site throw** the catch arm MUST emit the two-token `` `${code} <unreadable>` `` for `loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded` and the three-token `` `${code} ${entry.loom} <unreadable>` `` for `loom/runtime/cancelled-by-session-shutdown`. The forms exist specifically to preserve the per-`reason` and per-`loom` operator dedup buckets that bare-`code` collapse would destroy.

`V9g`'s and `V9g-T`'s **Tests** fields assert exactly one of these: the `DIAG-1` host-rows bullet ends "a serialiser throw degrades to the bare-`code` form." No bullet names the producing condition (serialiser throw vs construction-site throw) for the two-token or three-token forms, nor the codes that emit them. The two non-bare facets ship with no citing test.

The coverage-matrix `cka-29` row names `V9g` as the closing leaf for these very obligations ("the bare-`code` / two-token / three-token serialiser-throw fallback forms, the construction-site self-wrap …"). The `H5a` citing-test scan and `H5d` per-cell at-least-one check both go green because *a* `cka-29`/`DIAG-1` test exists — so the gate certifies the row closed while two of the three mandated wire forms are unverified. This is the split-facet "green gate, untested facet" failure mode the plan otherwise forbids.

The later `cka-29` coverage row (`7259777`, "resolve code-keyed obligation areas lack a canonical citable token") reaffirmed `V9g` as the closer for all three forms but did not add the missing facet tests; it widened the green-gate surface rather than introducing the gap.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Extend `V9g-T`'s **Tests** field (and mirror the same vector into `V9g`'s **Tests** field, which restates the tests-task assertions) so each non-bare fallback wire form carries its own facet-naming citing test, citing `diagnostic-emission-isolation.md`. Cover the producing conditions the spec distinguishes:

- **Serialiser-throw, two-token.** For each of the two nested-shape codes `loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded` and `loom/runtime/cancelled-by-session-shutdown`, force a serialiser throw and assert the emitted argument is `` `${code} ${detailsEventReason}` `` — the `details.event.reason` token preserved (including the `"<unreadable>"` sentinel on the sub-step-2 stamp-throw entry per the *Hoist obligation*).
- **Construction-site, two-token.** For `loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded`, force a `details.event` construction-site throw and assert the emitted argument is `` `${code} <unreadable>` ``.
- **Construction-site, three-token.** For `loom/runtime/cancelled-by-session-shutdown`, force a construction-site throw and assert the emitted argument is `` `${code} ${entry.loom} <unreadable>` `` — the `entry.loom` token read separately from the `ActiveInvocationRegistry` entry and the `details.event.reason` slot carrying `"<unreadable>"`.

Keep the existing bare-`code` serialiser-throw assertion. Update `V9g`'s **Ships when** so the driven shutdown observes the added forms alongside the per-step isolation and await-cap assertions it already names. Edge cases the test author must exercise: the construction-site and serialisation-and-emission paths are mutually exclusive (assert at most one emission per event/invocation, per *Count semantics*), and a `console.error` inner-throw on any fallback arm must be swallowed without a second emission.

## Consequence

**Severity:** correctness

Two reasonable implementers diverge: one wires all four catch-arm cases (serialiser-throw two-token, construction-site two-token, construction-site three-token, each preserving its dedup discriminator), another collapses every fallback to bare-`code` or omits the construction-site path entirely — and both pass `cka-29`/`DIAG-1` and ship `V9g-complete`. The dedup-bucket-preservation behaviour the spec spends its longest paragraph pinning is the silent casualty.
