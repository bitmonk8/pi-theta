## Problem

The depth-walk Tests bullets in `V5e` and `V5e-T` describe running the loom-owned JSON-depth walk "in isolation against the harness", but "the harness" is a reserved term naming the end-to-end test harness established by `H4a` (with its scripting surface owned by `H4b`). Neither leaf declares `H4a`/`H4b` in its `Deps`, while every other leaf that genuinely drives that harness does. The same bullets also state the test mirrors `V16a`'s seam pattern — and `V16a` exercises its decision standalone, depending on neither `H4a` nor `H4b`. The contradiction lets two reasonable implementers resolve `V5e`'s ordering in opposite directions: one gates the pure depth walk behind the entire extension shell and session double, the other builds it standalone.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** introduced-by-later-edit
**Introducing commit:** `3fa39a9` — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"V5e per-boundary routing test asserts destination error surfaces its Deps cannot reach\"" (2026-06-11)
**History:** The plan corpus is git-tracked. The original build of this leaf (`c6a664e`, "build/update plan for spec.md + review") had no "harness" phrasing: its Tests bullet read "A materialised value of depth 6 fires `schema_keyword:\"maxDepth\"` … at each of the five sites", i.e. it asserted at the live enforcement sites, and `Deps` were `V5e-T`, `V5d`, `V16a`. Commit `3fa39a9` refactored the Tests from live-site assertions to isolated assertions (resolving an unrelated "Deps cannot reach the destination surfaces" finding) and in doing so introduced the "in isolation against the harness" / "mirroring `V16a`'s seam pattern" phrasing — the point at which the reserved "harness" term collided with `Deps` that omit `H4a`/`H4b`. Commit `9d880b8` ("resolve \"V5e before-AJV Tests bullet asserts an ordering its isolated harness cannot witness\"", 2026-06-17) propagated the same "against the harness" phrasing to the remaining (depth-computation) bullet. The defect is single-commit in origin (`3fa39a9`) with later propagation (`9d880b8`); it was not present since inception.

## Solution approach

In both `docs/plan_topics/V5e-depth-enforcement.md` and `docs/plan_topics/V5e-T-depth-enforcement.md`, strike the reserved term "the harness" from the depth-walk Tests bullets, rewording the three occurrences of "in isolation against the harness" / "asserted in isolation against the harness" to "in isolation" — the standalone-seam phrasing `V16a` and `V5e`'s own Ships-when line already use. Leave the `V5e` and `V5e-T` `Deps` unchanged, since the depth walk is a pure function over a materialised value and does not consume the `H4a`/`H4b` session double.
