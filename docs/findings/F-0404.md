## Problem

`V15h` and its paired tests leaf `V15h-T` are the `invoke`-child entry in the four-site swallowing-handler routing set `V17a` delegates to its owning leaves. Like its siblings, `V15h` asserts that a late settlement landed via the `Checkpoint` seam (`V8a`) after the checkpoint surfaced `cause: "cancelled"` is suppressed along all three side channels — no Node `unhandledRejection`, no second `RuntimeEvent`, no diagnostic. Observing those absences requires the response-programming substrate that `H4b` provides, yet neither `V15h` (Deps `V15h-T`, `V15a`, `V5e`, `V8a`) nor `V15h-T` (Deps `V15a`, `V5e`, `V8a`) declares `H4b`, while every sibling carrying the same assertion (`V13f`, `V14f`, `V17a`) does. Without that edge the leaf can be implemented before `H4b` lands and its negative assertion can pass vacuously against an environment that never wired the observation substrate.

**Verdict:** single-commit introduction (born defective at the leaf split)
**Introducing commit:** `5f4d271` — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"V15a bundles ~14 independent test obligations into one impl+T pair\"" (2026-06-17 16:42:04 +0200)
**History:** The plan corpus is git-tracked (1487 commits). `git log --follow` shows both `docs/plan_topics/V15h-invoke-ceiling-swallowing.md` and `docs/plan_topics/V15h-T-invoke-ceiling-swallowing.md` were created in exactly one commit, `5f4d271`, which carved `V15h`/`V15h-T` out of `V15a` and (in the same commit) retargeted the four-site routing-set references in `V13f` and `V17a` from the old `V15a` site to the new `V15h` site. `git log -S '`H4b`'` over the V15h files returns no commit — the `H4b` edge was never present. The sibling leaves acquired their `H4b` edge in earlier, separate commits (`V13f` at `eaa5000`, `V14f` at `5a1915d`, `V17a` at `fd6169b`). The defect is therefore an omission at the moment `V15h` was created: the swallowing-handler routing role and its three-channel assertion moved onto the new leaf, but the `H4b` harness dependency that the role requires was not copied across.

## Solution approach

Add the `H4b` Deps edge to both V15h leaves: extend the `**Deps.**` field of `docs/plan_topics/V15h-invoke-ceiling-swallowing.md` and of `docs/plan_topics/V15h-T-invoke-ceiling-swallowing.md` to include `H4b`, bringing the invoke-child routing site into lockstep with its siblings `V13f` and `V14f` which carry `H4b` for the identical assertion.
