## Finding

Step 2 bundles "write the leaf" with "if it closes a coverage-matrix REQ-ID, add it to H5b's Deps" without a combined done-condition, so the H5b.Deps update can be skipped (caught only later by the H5d CI arm). Suggested direction: promote the H5b.Deps maintenance into a sub-step with its own exit condition ("…add it to H5b's Deps and confirm H5d's CI arm stays green").
