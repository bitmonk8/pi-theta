## Problem

`docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/unknown-reason-rule.md` carries a dedicated `## Acceptance criteria — \`"throw:<String(error)>"\` discriminator grammar` section listing per-fixture obligations for the throw-discriminator family, but the peer fixture floor for the `"literals-shape-invalid"` discriminator — the conformance-suite obligation that each of the four structural sub-cases gets its own fixture — sits inline at the end of the multi-thousand-word **Unknown-reason rule** body paragraph instead of under an Acceptance criteria heading. Both obligations are peers against the same closed `details.failure` discriminator set ([PIC-47] / substring (d) of [PIC-48]) and the same fixture-shape contract. A reader or plan-leaf author scanning the page for fixture obligations under `## Acceptance criteria` headings finds the throw-grammar fixtures and silently misses the buried `"literals-shape-invalid"` ones — the exact discoverability gap the Acceptance criteria section was carved out to prevent.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `a7d2a80` (Sun Jun 7 2026, `pi-loom spec: resolve "patch-skew teardown / literals-shape-invalid fixture enumeration"`) — added the four-sub-case fixture floor inline at the end of the **Unknown-reason rule** body paragraph; `8008b91` (Thu Jun 25 2026, `pi-loom spec-plan: fix F-1217 — add acceptance-criteria/fixture blocks for four Shard-08 MUSTs`) — added the `## Acceptance criteria — \`"throw:<String(error)>"\` discriminator grammar` section as a sibling of the body paragraph but did not migrate (or mirror) the pre-existing `"literals-shape-invalid"` four-sub-case fixture floor into a parallel section.
**History:** `git log -S'"literals-shape-invalid"' -- docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/unknown-reason-rule.md` and `git log -L8,16:docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/unknown-reason-rule.md` together pin the timeline: the `"literals-shape-invalid"` clause and its conformance-suite obligation entered the body paragraph in `a7d2a80` (`docs/spec.md` had been split out of by `f5e89f4` already), and the file had no `## Acceptance criteria` section until `8008b91` added the throw-discriminator one as a peer of the body paragraph. Neither commit individually introduced the placement defect: `a7d2a80` was authored when no Acceptance criteria section existed on the page, so inlining the fixture floor was structurally consistent; `8008b91` carved out an Acceptance criteria section but scoped its diff to the throw-discriminator family. The inconsistency is the cross-product of the two and surfaced the moment `8008b91` landed.

## Solution approach

Add a new `## Acceptance criteria` section for the `"literals-shape-invalid"` discriminator, peer to the existing `## Acceptance criteria — \`"throw:<String(error)>"\` discriminator grammar` section, surveying the four structural sub-cases' per-fixture obligations already stated in the body paragraph. Cite [PIC-47] and substring (d) of [PIC-48] so the new section keys on the same closed discriminator set.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: removing the four-sub-case enumeration and its conformance-suite obligation from the **Unknown-reason rule** body paragraph — the *Lookup-failure-to-discriminator routing* arm (4) forward-reference depends on it remaining there.
