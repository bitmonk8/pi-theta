## Finding

The `ERR-12` test bullet in `V4c-terminal-outcomes.md` ends with the clause
"…the live-surface re-assertion `V9i` carries off the `H4a` double." Two
elements of that clause have no established meaning at the bullet:

- **"carries off"** is not a defined relation. It reads equally as *replaces*,
  *performs alongside*, or *removes/relieves*, so a reader cannot tell what
  relationship `V9i` bears to the `H4a`-harness assertion.
- **"the `H4a` double"** is never glossed at this bullet. `H4a` builds an
  in-process Pi session double (named the "in-process double" in
  `real-host-smoke-gate.md`), but the ERR-12 bullet introduces the bare term
  "the `H4a` double" without that referent.

The intended meaning is recoverable only by reading the `V9i` leaf, which states
the relationship cleanly: "This is the live-surface confirmation `V4c` defers
from the `H4a` double" — i.e. `V9i` owns the live-surface re-assertion against
the real subagent `AgentSession`, exercised independently of the `H4a` in-process
double used under `npm test`. The V4c bullet should carry that meaning directly
rather than relying on a cross-leaf read. The paired `V4c-T` bullet already
phrases the same scope-exclusion crisply ("— not the live `V9i` surface").

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

The `ERR-12` `npm test` assertion itself (ERR-8 holds inside a subagent loom via
the `H4a` harness) is unambiguous, so no test author diverges on what to write.
The cost is reader confusion about ownership of the deferred live-surface
re-assertion and the meaning of "the `H4a` double" — an implementer reading V4c
in isolation cannot tell whether `V9i` duplicates, supplants, or merely relieves
the `H4a`-harness assertion.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Rewrite the trailing clause of the `ERR-12` bullet in
`docs/plan_topics/V4c-terminal-outcomes.md` so the `V9i` ownership and the
`H4a`-double referent are explicit at the bullet, mirroring the relationship the
`V9i` leaf already states. Replace the final segment beginning
"…criterion (c)), the live-surface re-assertion `V9i` carries off the `H4a`
double." with text equivalent to:

> "…witnessed at the manual real-host smoke gate
> ([`real-host-smoke-gate.md`](./real-host-smoke-gate.md) criterion (c)); that
> live-surface re-assertion against the real subagent `AgentSession` is owned by
> `V9i`, which exercises it independently of the `H4a` in-process session double
> used under `npm test`."

Keep the existing `npm test`-scoped assertion at the head of the bullet intact;
only the trailing delegation clause changes. `V4c-T`'s ERR-12 bullet already
carries the crisp exclusion phrasing and needs no change.
