## Problem

The `dispose()`/`abort()` overlap presupposition (anchor `#dispose-mid-abort-presupposition`) — that `AgentSession.dispose()` is safe to invoke while the cancellation listener's `abort()` is still unsettled, and that `abort()`'s idle-wait drives to completion through the subsequent `dispose()` within the `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS` budget — is an unpinned host-platform behavioural assumption sitting inline in the subagent teardown sequence rather than in the host-presupposition catalogue on `host-prerequisites.md`. That catalogue is the single home for loom's unpinned Pi behavioural presuppositions (degraded-state branch, settings write-back, operator-always-present, and the others); this one is the outlier left in the consuming teardown prose.
