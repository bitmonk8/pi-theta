## Problem

`host-prerequisites.md` carries a sub-section anchored `#clause-a-contradiction-narrative` whose own opening sentence classifies it as "decision-log narrative, not load-bearing normative text" and concedes a reader may skip it. It then walks the evidentiary investigation behind clause (a)'s open-contradiction classification (Pi's `docs/extensions.md` teardown-and-rebind lifecycle, the closed-over `LoomRegistry` survival argument, the CLAUDE.md no-globals foreclosure). The normative load is already carried elsewhere: the gate obligation in clause (a) of `#degraded-state-host-prerequisites`, and the recording contract in `version-bump-step2.md#bump-step-2-outcome-recording-conventions`. The narrative adds no requirement, so its presence forces every reader of the prerequisites contract to recognise and skip it.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit-of-introduction with subsequent expansion
**Introducing commits:**
- `7447d1e` (2026-06-10) — "pi-loom plan: resolve "Session-only degraded-state presupposition (a) contradicts Pi's documented lifecycle"" — rewrote clause (a) from a one-sentence unpinned presupposition into the "*— open contradiction against Pi's documented lifecycle*" paragraph that embeds the bulk of the investigation prose, and added the `bump-checklist-instance-survival` anchor in `version-bump-step2.md` with the "**Open at the loom 1.0 pin, not merely a future-minor re-audit**" preamble. This is the commit that introduced the embedded-TBD shape both sites now carry.
- `b8f004f` (2026-06-19) — "fix F-0719 — SM-4/5/6/3b degraded-state implementation-gate contingency markers" — added the *Implementation gate* blockquote at the head of `session-only-degraded-state.md`, the third citation site this finding flags.

This commit *partially* factored the decision-log out (the recording-shape contract now has a canonical home) but left the contradiction narrative inline at both consuming sites.

**History:** `git log --follow` over the cited paragraphs in `host-prerequisites.md` and `version-bump-step2.md`, together with `git log -S "bump-checklist-instance-survival"` and `git log -S "Open at the loom 1.0 pin"`, point to `7447d1e` as the single commit that introduced the embedded-decision-log shape; subsequent commits expanded the prose and partially relocated the recording-shape contract but did not relocate the contradiction narrative itself.

## Solution approach

Condense the `#clause-a-contradiction-narrative` sub-section in `host-prerequisites.md` to a brief evidentiary note, deleting the investigation walk-through. Keep the `docs/extensions.md` "Session replacement lifecycle and footguns" citation and the fresh non-drained `LoomRegistry` conclusion, since those are the evidentiary kernel editorial review consumes at each Pi minor bump.

## Solution constraints

- Retain the `#clause-a-contradiction-narrative` anchor so inbound cross-references from presupposition (a) and PIC-7 continue to resolve.
- Out of scope: introducing a separate decision-log or ADR file; the note stays inline on the page.
