## Problem

The clause-(a) open question — whether loom's package-loaded `extensions/index.ts` survives a session-only `session_shutdown` — is recorded with extensive investigation narrative at two normative sites: a multi-hundred-word body inside clause (a) of `host-prerequisites.md`'s `#degraded-state-host-prerequisites` section, and an "Open at the loom 1.0 pin" preamble on item (a) (`#bump-checklist-instance-survival`) of `version-bump-step2.md`'s per-bump audit checklist. The substantive recording-shape contract (two verdicts, evidentiary standard, acceptance criterion) has already been factored into the `#bump-step-2-outcome-recording-conventions` block, so what remains inline at both sites is decision-log narrative rather than load-bearing normative text. The oversized clause (a) breaks the crisp (a)–(d) presupposition list, and the oversized item (a) row breaks the uniform per-bump checklist shape; every future revision of the narrative must be applied and kept in sync across both sites.

## Solution approach

Make `host-prerequisites.md` the single home for the contradiction narrative and move clause (a)'s investigation prose out of the normative bullet body into a distinct, skippable sibling sub-section on the same page, leaving clause (a) itself as the gate statement plus a forward-link to that sub-section. Rewrite `version-bump-step2.md`'s item (a) preamble (`#bump-checklist-instance-survival`) to a pointer that names the item's one-shot authoritative-determination recording shape and forward-links `#bump-step-2-outcome-recording-conventions` for the recording contract and the new `host-prerequisites.md` sub-section for the narrative.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve the `degraded-state-host-prerequisites` and `bump-checklist-instance-survival` anchors verbatim — `plan.md`, `V9l-session-only-degraded-branch.md`, and `session-only-degraded-state.md`'s Implementation-gate blockquote cite them.
- Preserve the gate semantics unchanged: clause (a) remains MUST-resolve-before-SM-4/5/6 (and SM-3b), item (a) remains a one-shot authoritative-determination record rather than a recurring per-bump pass/fail row, and the two-verdict / evidentiary-standard / acceptance-criterion contract in `#bump-step-2-outcome-recording-conventions` is unchanged.
- Out of scope: `session-only-degraded-state.md`'s Implementation-gate blockquote.
