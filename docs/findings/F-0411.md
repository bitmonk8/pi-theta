## Problem

`V18b` (`docs/plan_topics/V18b-inventory-audit.md`) packs two verification
tasks of different character into one leaf. The first is constructing the
inventory-closure audit walker (the category-(1)/(2)/(3) recogniser, the
non-exemptible family-(4) discriminator, the `audit/<class>/<family>/<symptom>`
record grammar, the fail-closed `npm test` wiring, and the non-empty canary).
The second is the land-green `src/` sweep the `Adds` assigns to the
audit-introducing commit: every pre-existing `src/` Pi-side reference the new
audit would surface must be deleted, promoted, exempted, or rewritten so the
gate lands green on `main`. The two have unrelated review surfaces and the
sweep's site count is unbounded, so a single review pass and single completion
gate over both cannot verify either coherently at a non-trivial violation
count. This does not block execution — the defect is leaf granularity, not
commit granularity.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** introduced-by-later-edit (multi-commit interaction)
**Introducing commits:** `c6a664e` (2026-06-10, "build/update plan for spec.md + review") created `V18b` carrying the audit-walker construction; `402bdbe` (2026-06-11, "resolve \"V18b Adds omits broader-inventory population, taxonomy, and land-green sweep\"") grafted the land-green `src/` sweep ownership onto the same leaf.
**History:** `git log --follow` on `docs/plan_topics/V18b-inventory-audit.md` shows two commits. The leaf was born at inception (`c6a664e`) with only the walker/recogniser/canary/fail-closed obligations. The `git show 402bdbe` diff adds the entire "This leaf also owns the land-green `src/` sweep …" clause to `Adds`. The "two tasks in one leaf" condition this finding flags therefore did not exist at inception; it materialised at `402bdbe`, when a prior review fix correctly pulled the spec-mandated sweep into `V18b` (closing a coverage omission) but, in doing so, doubled the leaf's review surface.

## Solution approach

Restructure `V18b`'s `Adds` and `Ships when` so the walker-construction concern
and the land-green sweep concern each carry their own observable, letting a
reviewer verify them in separate passes — without changing the leaf boundary,
the DAG, or the commit shape. Give the sweep its own completeness observable
distinct from the walker's "red against a seeded reference" observable, and
confirm the non-empty canary witnesses sweep completeness over the full audited
tree.

## Solution constraints

- The spec topic files (`inventory-audit-intro.md` and its siblings) are read-only for this fix.
- Preserve the same-commit, land-green obligation in `inventory-audit-intro.md` §Inventory-closure audit: the audit and its `src/` sweep land in one commit; no land-red / fix-incrementally gate.
