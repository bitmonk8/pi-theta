## Problem

The *System-prompt structure (normative)* subsection of `binder/binder-bypass-and-envelope.md` enumerates eight independent `MUST`-bearing obligations (items 1–8) as a plain numbered list, but none of the eight carries a co-located REQ-ID or page-local anchor — the only stable identifier is the section-heading anchor `#system-prompt-structure-normative`, which addresses all eight as a unit. Every inbound reference to a single item therefore resolves it by ordinal position rather than stable id: `binder-model-and-context.md` cites "item 6 below", "item 5", and the BNDR-7 renderings cite "item 6" by number. Positional citation breaks silently when an item is inserted or reordered, and four of the items govern independent conditional-presence rules that a conformance test or coverage facet needs to cite individually. GOV-22/GOV-9 require these MUST-bearing sites to carry `#prefix-n` anchors, and the coverage-matrix row `cka-45` already flags the section as un-anchored GOV-22 residue.

## Solution approach

Then add anchor links replacing the positional cross-references in `binder-model-and-context.md` ("item 6 below", "item 5", and the BNDR-7 "item 6" reference) so each resolves to the corresponding per-item anchor rather than by ordinal.
