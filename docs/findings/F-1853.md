## Finding

The `retryable` rule covers network-level, 5xx, 429, other-4xx, and 200-body-error; a resolved status outside 2xx/4xx/5xx (e.g. 1xx/3xx via `onResponse` with `stopReason: "error"`) matches no arm and leaves `retryable` unassigned.

**Suggested fix:** Add a residual clause (any resolved status not covered → `retryable: false`), or explicitly declare it unreachable with the reason (adapters follow redirects).
