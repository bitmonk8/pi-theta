## Finding

Both `V13b` and `V13b-T` list only `query.md` and `query/query-forms.md` in their **Spec.** field, yet each carries a Tests bullet that directly closes the `schema-subset.md` Lowering-Algorithm-step-4 obligation (SUBS code-keyed area: per-query `$defs` pruning to the definitions transitively reachable from the response-schema root). `schema-subset.md` is therefore a page these leaves implement, but it is not listed.

`conventions.md` §Leaf format states the **Spec** field MUST be closed under normative cross-link — closure runs outbound from each listed topic to a fixed point. Neither `query.md` nor `query/query-forms.md` cross-links to `schema-subset.md` (verified: no occurrence of `schema-subset` in either page), so `schema-subset.md` is not reachable by transitive closure from the listed pages. The closure invariant is broken, not merely under-documented.

The convention also grants that "an implementer MAY restrict their reading to these pages." An implementer exercising that license on `V13b`/`V13b-T` would never open `schema-subset.md`, leaving the per-query `$defs`-pruning algorithm out of their reading scope while the leaf is nonetheless required to test it.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit-introduced
**Introducing commit:** ba7da00 — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"V5d lowering-pass outputs bound by no Tests bullet\"" (Thomas Andersen, 2026-06-10)
**History:** At the leaves' inception (c6a664e) `V13b`/`V13b-T` listed only `query.md` and `query/query-forms.md` in Spec and carried no `schema-subset.md` obligation (Tests had two bullets; Deps `V13a`, `V2b`). Commit ba7da00 added the `schema-subset.md` Lowering-Algorithm-step-4 (SUBS) Tests bullet and the `V5d` Deps edge to both leaves (the `V5f` Deps edge was added subsequently) but left the **Spec** field unchanged, introducing the closure gap. The Tests obligation and the Spec listing have been out of step since that commit.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Add `schema-subset.md` to the **Spec.** line of both `docs/plan_topics/V13b-query-schema-inference.md` and `docs/plan_topics/V13b-T-query-schema-inference.md`, e.g. append `, [`../spec_topics/schema-subset.md`](../spec_topics/schema-subset.md)` to the existing `query.md` / `query-forms.md` listing so the page bearing the tested SUBS obligation is in the Spec field's normative-closure set. No spec edit is required — `schema-subset.md` already exists and already carries the Lowering-Algorithm-step-4 obligation the Tests bullet cites; the fix is confined to the two plan leaf pages.

## Consequence

**Severity:** correctness

The Spec-field normative-closure MUST in `conventions.md` is violated: a directly-tested obligation lives on a page that is neither listed nor reachable by closure from the listed pages. An implementer who relies on the convention's explicit "MAY restrict reading to these pages" license omits `schema-subset.md` and may implement the per-query `$defs` pruning incompletely or incorrectly.
