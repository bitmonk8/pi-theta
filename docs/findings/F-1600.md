## Problem

The Pi version-bump procedure is a single numbered checklist split across four topic pages. The full text of numbered steps 3 ("Re-confirm the `engines.node` floor") and 4 ("Update the version pin in `peerDependencies` and the equivalent literal here") is appended to the tail of `version-bump-step2b.md`, whose H1 is `# Version bump step2b` and whose sole hub-ToC entry reads `Version bump step2b` — both signalling step-2(b) content only. A contributor navigating by step number, or scanning the ToC, finds `step2b` then `Version bump steps 5–7` with no page named for the intervening steps, so steps 3 and 4 are invisible to step-number and ToC navigation even though their text is present. The content of steps 3 and 4 is correct and complete; only their location is wrong.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commit:** `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04) — "docs: enforce 100KB hard / 30KB recommended size cap on spec set"
**History:** The `step 2(a)` / `step 2(b)` textual designations predate the split — `git show f5e89f4^:docs/spec.md` and the pre-split `pi-integration-contract.md` already carry the `step 2(a)` form, so the designation namespace was not introduced by this commit. `f5e89f4` is the commit that, to satisfy the size cap, split the consolidated version-bump prose out of `pi-integration-contract.md` into the child files `version-bump-intro.md`, `version-bump-step2.md`, `version-bump-step2b.md`, and `version-bump-triggers.md` (`git log --diff-filter=A --follow` dates the creation of both `version-bump-step2.md` and `version-bump-step2b.md` to `f5e89f4`, and `git show f5e89f4:…/version-bump-step2b.md` shows the `# Version bump step2b` title already present at creation). The defect is the choice, at that split, of a letter-suffix overflow naming scheme (`step2` + `step2b`) that collides with the older `(a)`/`(b)` designation namespace; it is attributable to that single commit rather than to a multi-commit interaction.

## Solution approach

Move the text of numbered steps 3 and 4 out of the tail of `version-bump-step2b.md` into a new, correctly-titled topic file under `docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/`, leaving `version-bump-step2b.md` ending at step 2(b)'s branch (5) (`#bump-step-2b-rewrite-shape`). Add a hub ToC entry for the new file in `pi-integration-contract.md` between the `Version bump step2b` and `Version bump steps 5–7` entries. Repoint every file-anchored citation to "step 3 / step 4 in `version-bump-step2b.md`" — notably in `V18c-version-bump-checklist.md` — to the new file, and rewrite the relocated steps' same-page relative references (e.g. step 4's "at the top of this page", and "[Step 0 (a)] above") into resolving cross-links.
