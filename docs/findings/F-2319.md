## Problem

`H4a` bundles four reasoning-independent deliverables in one leaf: the production Pi extension factory (the `src/**` implementation, the `extensions/index.ts` delegating shim, and the synchronous-arm never-throw factory-boundary contract); the end-to-end test harness with its in-process session double and in-memory fixture-supply mechanism; the four-axis session-double fidelity contract and its harness self-check; and a minimal response-emission capability. The leaf's `Tests.` section already exposes the seam, carrying three separate `Convention:` bullets — each an independently-failing property. Item 1 is production code; items 2–4 are test-only infrastructure, coupled only by co-location rather than by a shared reasoning span. The wide `Deps: H4a` consumer set inherits the whole bundle as a prerequisite even when a consumer needs only the production factory or only the harness.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception (widened by later accretion)
**Introducing commits:** `c6a664e` (2026-06-10, plan inception) — co-located the production factory entry, the end-to-end harness, the session double, and the fidelity contract in a single `H4a` leaf; `32ea081` (2026-06-20, fix F-0477) — added the minimal response-emission capability to `H4a` to make the fidelity self-check self-contained.
**History:** `H4a` has coupled the production factory boundary with the test harness / session-double / fidelity-contract since the plan was first built (`c6a664e`). A prior atomicity fix — `fd6169b` (2026-06-12, "H4a bundles three independent units into one leaf") — extracted the full response-programming surface to `H4b` and retargeted consumers, but left the production-factory ↔ test-harness coupling intact. `F-0477` (`32ea081`) subsequently re-loaded `H4a` with the minimal response-emission capability, producing the current four-concern bundle. The defect is a standing structural coupling present since inception, widened by later leaf-edits rather than introduced by any single one.

## Solution approach

Split `H4a` along the production/test boundary. Retain in `H4a` the production factory — the `src/**` implementation, the `extensions/index.ts` delegating shim, the synchronous-arm / never-throw factory-boundary contract, the factory-body registration/subscription enumeration, and the factory `Convention:` Tests bullet. Add a new H4-phase harness leaf (`Deps: H4a`) carrying the end-to-end harness, the in-process session double, the in-memory fixture-supply mechanism, the four-axis fidelity contract with its self-check, and the minimal response-emission capability, moving the end-to-end-dispatch and fidelity-self-check `Convention:` Tests bullets and the corresponding `Ships when` clause to it; link it into `docs/plan.md`'s Horizontal phases list after `H4a`. Retarget each current `H4a` consumer to whichever leaf it needs: factory-only consumers keep `Deps: H4a`, while consumers that load or drive the harness or session double (`H4b`, `H7a`, `M`/`M-T`, `V4c`, `V4f`, `V7a`, `V7d`, `V9a`, `V9k`, `V9p`, `V17a`/`V17b`/`V17c`, `V18d`, and their `-T` partners) depend on the new leaf.

## Solution constraints

- Allocate the new leaf ID per the `H<n><letter>` scheme in `## Plan rules` — next free letter in the H4 phase (`H4a`/`H4b`/`H4c` are live, so `H4d`); never reuse a retired ID.
- Preserve DAG acyclicity when retargeting the broad consumer set; the new leaf's `Deps: H4a` means any consumer pointed at it transitively retains `H4a`.
- Keep `docs/plan_topics/conventions.md` and `docs/plan_topics/coverage-matrix.md` consistent with the split per `## Plan rules`.
