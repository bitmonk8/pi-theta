## Finding

`V17a` is a *consumer* of `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS`, which `V9a` declares and exports. Yet `V17a`'s **Adds** restates three pieces of ownership metadata that belong solely on the owning leaf `V9a`:

1. A code-location mandate — the constant "physically resides in the single source-of-truth pinned-constants block established by `V9a`."
2. An enumeration of the other consumers — `V9g`'s session-shutdown await cap and `V9i`'s subagent-disposal bound.
3. A normative single-declaration constraint directed at those sibling leaves — "no leaf redeclares it."

`V9a`'s **Adds** already carries all three: it exports the constant, names `V9g`, `V9i`, and `V17a` as importers, and states they "import rather than redeclare." `V17a`'s restatement is therefore duplicate ownership prose living on a consumer leaf. `V17a` legitimately owns only the *runtime semantics* of the constant and its own consumption of `V9a`'s export; the placement, the cross-leaf consumer roster, and the single-declaration rule are `V9a`'s to state.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** 622d0d3 — pi-loom plan: resolve "SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS physical home contradicts pinned-constants-block placement" (2026-06-11, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** Before 622d0d3, `V17a` was the declared owner of `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS` and its Adds named `V9g`/`V9i` as consumers that do not redeclare it. Commit 622d0d3 relocated the constant's physical home to `V9a` (adding `V9a` to `V17a`'s `Deps`) but left the source-of-truth/placement clause, the `V9g`/`V9i` consumer enumeration, and the "no leaf redeclares it" constraint on `V17a`'s Adds, so they now duplicate the ownership statement that `V9a` carries.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/V17a-cancellation-core.md`, reduce the `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS` clause in **Adds** to a consumption reference: state that `V17a` consumes `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS` as exported by `V9a` and owns its cancellation-runtime semantics (value sourced from `session-shutdown-semantics.md` §`session_shutdown` sub-step 3).

Strike from `V17a`'s **Adds**:

- the clause "The constant itself physically resides in the single source-of-truth pinned-constants block established by `V9a` … so `V18c`'s build-time literal-read assertion can reach it";
- the `V9g`/`V9i` consumer enumeration ("`V9g`'s session-shutdown await cap and `V9i`'s subagent-disposal bound consume that same block constant");
- the "no leaf redeclares it" single-declaration constraint.

`V9a`'s **Adds** already states the export, the `V9g`/`V9i`/`V17a` consumer roster, and the import-rather-than-redeclare rule, so it requires no edit. Retain `V17a`'s `Deps` on `V9a`.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

The ownership/placement/no-redeclare statements now live on both `V17a` and `V9a` and can drift out of sync under future edits, with no rule pinning `V9a` as the authoritative copy. The consumer leaf also carries a code-location mandate and a normative constraint directed at sibling leaves that is not its to make; the behavioural contract — `V17a` depends on `V9a`, and `V9a` is the single declarer — is already fully expressed by `V17a`'s `Deps` on `V9a` and by `V9a`'s `Adds`.
