## Problem

`V12a`/`V12a-T`'s `SLSH-2` streaming-ordering test consumes `H4b`'s response-programming surface (category (a) per-turn streamed fragments; category (e) cancellation injection for the mid-stream-cancel arm) and `H4a`'s session-double fidelity contract with its ordering-observable transcript, but the dependency is expressed only in `V12a`'s `SLSH-2` prose, not in any `Deps` field the dep-driven build order reads. Neither `V12a` (`Deps. V12a-T, V9c, V11f, V4a, V13c`) nor `V12a-T` (`Deps. V9c, V11f, V4a, V13c`) reaches `H4a`/`H4b`, and `H4b`'s `Adds` consumer enumeration omits `V12a`. Every other harness-driven leaf carries `H4b` in `Deps` (and the streaming/cancellation-shaped ones additionally carry `H4a`). As a result `V12a`/`V12a-T` are pickable before the harness contract exists, so the implementer invents a bespoke double and a local transcript sink and asserts streamed-token ordering against ordering they themselves defined — a tautology rather than a fidelity-pinned assertion.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `eeb0014` — "pi-loom plan-review: park T20 (fast-loop: partial — V18c backstop hollow, needs human judgment)" (2026-06-10); `fd6169b` — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"H4a bundles three independent units into one leaf\"" (2026-06-12)
**History:** At the initial plan build `c6a664e` (2026-06-10 08:16) both `H4a` and `V12a` were authored; `V12a`'s `Deps` was set to `V12a-T, V9c, V11f, V4a, V13c` (no `H4a`), and its `SLSH-2` bullet already referenced the "in-process Pi session double" and "ordering-observable transcript sink" without an explicit fidelity-contract citation. `eeb0014` (2026-06-10 10:20) sharpened the `SLSH-2` prose to explicitly cite the `H4a` "session-double fidelity contract" and "the ordering asserted here is the one that contract requires the double to model" — making the dependency on `H4a` explicit in prose — but left the `Deps` field unchanged (`git log -S "session-double fidelity contract" -- docs/plan_topics/V12a-slash-dispatch.md` confirms `eeb0014` is the sole commit that introduced the explicit fidelity-contract reference). `H4b` did not exist as a separate leaf until `fd6169b` (2026-06-12 08:17) split it out of `H4a`; that split created `H4b`'s consumer enumeration as `H7a, V4c, V9i, V11f, V13c, V13d, V17a` (`git show fd6169b -- docs/plan_topics/H4b-response-programming-surface.md`), already omitting `V12a`, and subsequent expansions that appended `V4f`, `V13f`, and `V14b` never added it. The defect as it now stands is the joint product of the prose-only dependency sharpened at `eeb0014` and the consumer-list omission introduced at `fd6169b`; neither commit carried the matching `Deps`/consumer edge.

## Solution approach

Add `H4a` and `H4b` to the `Deps` field of both `docs/plan_topics/V12a-slash-dispatch.md` and `docs/plan_topics/V12a-T-slash-dispatch.md`, mirroring the other streaming/cancellation-shaped harness-driven leaves. Add `V12a` to `H4b`'s `Adds` consumer enumeration alongside the existing consumers. In both leaves' `SLSH-2` text, clarify that the streamed-fragment scripting and the ordering-observable transcript are consumed from the harness — `H4b`'s response-programming surface and `H4a`'s fidelity contract — rather than a `V12a`-local double or sink.

## Solution constraints

- The ordering-observable transcript sink stays owned by `H4a`'s fidelity contract and its harness self-check (i); `V12a` consumes it and defines no local double or sink.
- Out of scope: the shared-`SLSH-2`-identifier issue owned by F-0392 (it resolves independently in the same `V12a` `SLSH-2` block).
