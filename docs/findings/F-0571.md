## Problem

`V15h` and its paired `V15h-T` route the `invoke`-child execution Promise through the `Checkpoint`-seam swallowing-handler substrate that `V17a` introduces and owns, and their test asserts three-channel suppression "so a build that bypasses the substrate reddens this leaf's tests" ‚Äî an assertion that cannot be constructed before `V17a` lands. Yet neither leaf names `V17a` in its `Deps` (`V15h`: `V15h-T`, `V15a`, `V8a`; `V15h-T`: `V15a`, `V8a`). Under the pickability predicate, both become pickable once `V15a`/`V8a` complete, green-lighting `V15h` before the substrate exists. The sibling `V9o`/`V9o-T` declares the `V17a` edge correctly; `V15h` does not.

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** `5f4d2711c61f2e938df3d8fe40deceb169fa441d` (2026-06-17, "pi-loom plan: resolve \"V15a bundles ~14 independent test obligations into one impl+T pair\"")
**History:** `V15h-invoke-ceiling-swallowing.md` and its paired `V15h-T` page were created together in `5f4d271` with `Deps` of `V15h-T, V15a, V5e, V8a` (impl) and `V15a, V5e, V8a` (tests) ‚Äî already omitting `V17a` while the `Adds` prose referenced the `V17a` four-site routing set. The later edit `323d36169ce7abd2c4b1a56ab63eca5fd21f0f3a` (2026-06-18, "resolve \"V15h bundles two orthogonal failure modes\"") removed `V5e` but never added `V17a`. `git log -G "Deps.*V17a"` over the file returns no commit: `V17a` has never appeared in `V15h`'s `Deps`. The omission dates to the leaf's inception, not a later regression.

## Solution approach

Add `V17a` to the `Deps` field of both `docs/plan_topics/V15h-invoke-ceiling-swallowing.md` and `docs/plan_topics/V15h-T-invoke-ceiling-swallowing.md`, matching the `V9o`/`V9o-T` exemplar that already declares the edge.
