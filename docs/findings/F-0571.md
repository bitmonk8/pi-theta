## Finding

`V15h` (Invoke-child execution-Promise swallowing-handler per-site routing) is one of the four per-site routing leaves that `V17a` (Cancellation core) delegates to. `V17a` owns the `Checkpoint`-seam swallowing-handler substrate and explicitly states that the per-site routing property "is asserted by each owning leaf (`V14f`, `V13f`, `V15h`, `V9o` respectively)". `V15h`'s own `Adds` and `Tests` describe routing the `invoke`-child execution Promise *through that substrate*, and its test asserts three-channel suppression "so a build that bypasses the substrate reddens this leaf's tests" — a test that cannot be constructed before the substrate `V17a` introduces exists.

Despite that hard dependency on the substrate, `V15h`'s `Deps` are `V15h-T`, `V15a`, `V8a` and `V15h-T`'s `Deps` are `V15a`, `V8a` — neither names `V17a`. The sibling subagent-mode leaf `V9o` declares the edge correctly: `V9o` lists `V17a` in `Deps` and `V9o-T` lists `V17a` as well. Under the pickability predicate (a `Deps` entry is satisfied when its `-complete` tag is present), `V15h` and `V15h-T` become pickable once `V15a`/`V8a` are complete, even when `V17a`'s substrate has not yet landed.

The same omission is present in the two other site-routing siblings: `V14f` (`Deps`: `V14f-T`, `V14a`, `V8a`, `H4b`) and `V13f` (`Deps`: `V13f-T`, `V9n`, `V8a`, `H4b`) both route through the `V17a`-owned substrate yet omit `V17a`. Only `V9o`/`V9o-T` declare the edge. Adding `V17a` to `V15h`'s `Deps` introduces no cycle: `V17a`'s `Deps` are `V17a-T`, `V8a`, `V4d`, `V9a`, `H4a`, `H4b` and never reference any of the four site leaves.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Add `V17a` to the `Deps` field of `docs/plan_topics/V15h-invoke-ceiling-swallowing.md` (becoming `V15h-T`, `V15a`, `V17a`, `V8a`) and to `docs/plan_topics/V15h-T-invoke-ceiling-swallowing.md` (becoming `V15a`, `V17a`, `V8a`), matching the exemplar `V9o`/`V9o-T` which already declare the edge. This makes the substrate dependency explicit so the pickability predicate cannot green-light `V15h` before `V17a` lands.

The identical omission in the `V14f`/`V14f-T` and `V13f`/`V13f-T` siblings should be reconciled in the same pass (each adds `V17a` to its `Deps`), per the cross-shard note. No cycle results — `V17a`'s `Deps` do not reference any of the four site leaves.

## Consequence

**Severity:** correctness

With `V17a` absent from `V15h`/`V15h-T` `Deps`, the pickability predicate green-lights `V15h` as soon as `V15a`/`V8a` complete, before the `V17a` swallowing-handler substrate exists. An implementer who picks `V15h` early cannot construct its defining assertion ("a build that bypasses the substrate reddens this leaf's tests") because there is no substrate to route through; two implementers diverge on whether to sequence `V17a` first. The same hazard applies to the `V14f` and `V13f` siblings.
