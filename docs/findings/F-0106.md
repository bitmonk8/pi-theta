## Problem

The `V16a` / `V16a-T` leaf pair names the `masked` enumeration — and the multi-precondition input state it derives from — with three unreconciled terms: "co-fire"/"co-fired" (leaf title, CIO-6), "co-satisfied" (the Adds output bullet and the "Verified at the seam in isolation" bullet), and "co-present"/"co-presents" (CIO-5, CIO-1, and the witnesses sentence). The same noun — the sibling ceilings recorded in `masked` — is called both "co-satisfied sibling(s)" in Adds and "co-fired siblings" in CIO-6 within one leaf pair, with no inline equivalence declared. The spec pins a canonical term: PIC-1 in `pi-integration-contract/runtime-event-channel.md` owns "Hard-ceiling co-fire (`masked`)", so a reader searching the corpus on the spec's canonical "co-fire" hits the title but misses the seam's own output definition, and vice versa.

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction (term accretion)
**Introducing commits:** c6a664e (2026-06-10), e2b7e81 (2026-06-10), 8ef7055 (2026-06-11)
**History:** The plan corpus is git-tracked. `git log -S` over `V16a-ceiling-order-masked.md` / `V16a-T-ceiling-order-masked.md` shows the three terms entered in successive edits, none reconciling its predecessors: "co-fire"/"co-fired" was present at leaf inception (c6a664e, "build/update plan for spec.md + review"); "co-satisfied" was added by e2b7e81 ("resolve 'isolated cross-ceiling unit interface/authority undefined'") when the arbitration-seam Adds output was authored; "co-present" was added by 8ef7055 ("resolve 'V16a over-claims all CIO bullets provable at stateless seam'") when the verified/not-witnessable bullets were reworded. The three-way collision became complete at 8ef7055.

## Solution approach

Standardise the leaf pair on the spec's canonical "co-fire" vocabulary for the `masked` output enumeration and on a single term for the input candidate state. In `V16a`'s Adds output bullet, rename "co-satisfied sibling(s)" to "co-fired sibling(s)", matching the title, CIO-6, and the spec's "hard-ceiling co-fire enumeration". In `V16a`'s "Verified at the seam in isolation" bullet, rename "co-satisfied candidates" to "co-present candidates" so the input candidate state is named "co-present" uniformly (already its form in CIO-1, CIO-5, and the witnesses sentence). The title, CIO-6, and `V16a-T`'s CIO-5/CIO-6 already carry the canonical forms.
