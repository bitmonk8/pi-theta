## Problem

In `docs/spec_topics/discovery/package-and-settings.md`, the `looms.scanPackages` bullet under "Keys read" closes its rationale by stating that the defaults of "the three `looms.scanPackages*` keys" are *upper bounds*, not target performance. That framing is correct only for the two integer caps (`looms.scanPackagesMaxFiles`, `looms.scanPackagesTimeoutMs`); `looms.scanPackages` is a boolean on/off switch whose default `true` bounds nothing. Sweeping the boolean into the "upper bounds" claim makes the sentence false for one of the three keys it names and invites confusion about whether `scanPackages` caps something. The broader operator-tunability rationale legitimately covers all three keys.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit introduction
**Introducing commits:** 52219c4 (2026-05-06, "Address all 41 spec-review findings")
**History:** `git log -G "three .looms\.scanPackages"` shows the sentence first appearing in 52219c4, which addressed a separate spec-review finding that asked for a rationale paragraph explaining why the package-walk bounds were operator-tunable in loom 1.0. That finding's edge-case guidance (paraphrased in the commit diff: "the defaults are *upper bounds*, not target performance — i.e. a healthy install completes the walk well under both caps") was correctly worded only for the two integer caps; the implementing edit attached the same clause to the `looms.scanPackages` boolean bullet that introduces the rationale, sweeping all three `looms.scanPackages*` keys under it. The later 100 KB-cap split (f5e89f4, 2026-06-04) and subsequent maintenance edits to `discovery/package-and-settings.md` carried the sentence forward verbatim without revisiting the boolean/integer mismatch.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the trailing rationale sentence of the `looms.scanPackages` bullet so the *upper bounds*, not-target-performance framing applies only to the two integer caps (`looms.scanPackagesMaxFiles`, `looms.scanPackagesTimeoutMs`), while the operator-tunability rationale continues to cover all three `looms.scanPackages*` keys.
