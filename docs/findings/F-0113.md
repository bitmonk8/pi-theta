## Problem

`V18a`'s display title is "SDK capability inventory", but the leaf's **Adds** owns three things: the `CAPABILITY_OBLIGATIONS` constant (the seven-capability subset), the strictly-broader `SDK_SURFACE_INVENTORY` constant (the full Pi-side surface set, including non-capability rows), and the inventory's entry-kind taxonomy. The title surfaces only the first. `SDK_SURFACE_INVENTORY` and its taxonomy are the Class-2 seam that `V18b` (inventory-closure audit) and `V18c` (version-bump static gates) resolve against, so a reader searching for the owner of that seam sees a title scoped to "capability inventory" and may not recognise `V18a` as the owner.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `8d41813` (2026-06-11, "resolve 'Non-capability SDK_SURFACE_INVENTORY rows have no owning leaf'"), `402bdbe` (2026-06-11, "resolve 'V18b Adds omits broader-inventory population, taxonomy, and land-green sweep'")
**History:** The title "SDK capability inventory" is present since the leaf's inception (`c6a664e`, 2026-06-10), where `V18a` owned `CAPABILITY_OBLIGATIONS` plus a then-narrow `SDK_SURFACE_INVENTORY`. The leaf's scope was broadened — non-capability `SDK_SURFACE_INVENTORY` rows added in `8d41813`, the entry-kind taxonomy ownership added in `402bdbe` — without retitling. The naming mismatch emerged from the interaction between the frozen title and the two scope-broadening edits, not from any single commit.

## Solution approach

Rename the display title of the `V18a` leaf pair so it covers the full surface scope the leaf owns (the capability subset plus the broader surface inventory and taxonomy) rather than only the capability subset — e.g. "SDK capability and surface inventory". Apply the same retitle in all three sites that carry it: the H1 of `V18a-capability-inventory.md`, the H1 of `V18a-T-capability-inventory.md` (tests), and the V18 entry link text in `docs/plan.md` §Vertical slices. Keep the word "capability" so the link to the spec's PIC-15 section ("SDK capability inventory") survives.

## Solution constraints

- The spec is read-only for this fix — do not rename the spec section `inventory-audit-intro.md` §"SDK capability inventory".
- This is a display-title change only — do not change the `V18a` leaf ID or rename the `capability-inventory` file slug (Deps citations reference the ID, not the title).
