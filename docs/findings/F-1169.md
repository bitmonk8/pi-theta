## Problem

The syntactic surface of a `match` arm is split across two files. The Grammar Appendix (`grammar.md`), which owns every other surface-syntax production, devotes its `## `match` arm body` section to the `MatchArm` / `ArmBody` productions only and forwards via prose to a different file. The actual Pattern grammar — the six-row pattern table, the lowercase-binds-vs-capitalised-constructor disambiguation, and the `pattern => expression` arm-syntax sentence with its trailing-comma rule — lives in `error-model.md` (`**Pattern grammar (loom 1.0).**` / `**Arm syntax.**`), interleaved with semantic prose. The `grammar.md` forward link and the two `loom/parse/match-guard-not-supported` / `loom/parse/rest-pattern-not-supported` diagnostic-registry rows both point at the `errors-and-results.md` index page rather than at the production.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** 53f5831 — spec: rewrite query primitive, system prompts, and schema syntax (2026-05-03, Thomas Andersen); f841b12 — spec: resolve Cluster 2 (literal sublanguage + grammar appendix) (2026-05-04, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The `**Pattern grammar (V1).**` six-row table, disambiguation, exhaustiveness, and arm-syntax block first landed in the monolithic `spec.md` under `### Errors and Results` in 53f5831. The next day, f841b12 introduced the Grammar Appendix in response to spec-review finding "Missing normative grammar appendix (#17)" and moved a `## `match` arm body` production into it — but the Pattern grammar was left in the errors section, so the appendix shipped covering the arm body alone with a forward link back to errors. The subsequent `fecb504` topic split and `f5e89f4` 100KB shard only relocated the two halves into their current files (`spec_topics/grammar.md` and `spec_topics/errors-and-results/error-model.md`); neither reunified them.

## Solution approach

Move the syntactic Pattern grammar from `error-model.md` to `grammar.md`, co-located with the existing `## `match` arm body` section, and mint a stable anchor at the new site. Leave the semantic prose in `error-model.md` (exhaustiveness / runtime `MatchError`, the guard- and rest-pattern deferral, the per-arm LUB rule), replacing the moved blocks with a forward link to the new grammar.md anchor. Repoint the `let`-form forward link in `grammar.md` (currently `[Errors and Results — Pattern grammar](./errors-and-results.md)`) and the `loom/parse/match-guard-not-supported` / `loom/parse/rest-pattern-not-supported` "Spec section" cells in `code-registry-parse.md` to that anchor.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the per-arm LUB / `loom/parse/match-arm-type-mismatch` clause inside `error-model.md`'s `**Arm syntax.**` sentence — it is semantic, stays in `error-model.md`, and is owned by the "sink" same-cluster finding.
