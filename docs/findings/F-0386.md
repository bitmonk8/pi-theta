## Problem

The **Deps** fields of `V9l` and `V9l-T` each carry plan-governance prose rather than leaf-ID prerequisites: `V9l`'s Deps ends with a pickability rule ("blocked until the version-bump editorial-review checklist item (a) ... resolves the host-prerequisites clause (a) ... not pickable while the contradiction stands"), and `V9l-T`'s Deps carries a paired test-authoring risk statement. `conventions.md` §Leaf format defines Deps as "Other leaf IDs that must be complete first," and `plan.md` §Blocked obligations declares it owns the gate "not by leaf prose," so this content contradicts the section meant to own it. Compounding this, §Blocked obligations lists only `V9l` and omits its equally-blocked tests partner `V9l-T`, and the risk statement unique to `V9l-T` lives nowhere in the canonical section.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** `55ed974` (2026-06-12) "pi-loom plan: resolve 'degraded-state obligations green over open spec contradiction'"
**History:** `git log --follow` shows both `V9l` and `V9l-T` leaf files were created in `55ed974`, and `git log -S "not pickable while the contradiction stands"` / `git log -S "risks asserting contradicted behaviour"` localize both prose strings to that same commit. The §Blocked obligations section in `plan.md` was also authored in `55ed974` (`git log -G "Blocked obligations"`), listing only `V9l` from the outset. A later commit `9c42f2d` (2026-06-16, "V9l fallback path belongs in §Blocked obligations") refined the fallback-path placement but did not strip the Deps prose or add `V9l-T`. The defect has been present in both leaves since their inception.

## Solution approach

Move the governance prose out of the `V9l` and `V9l-T` Deps fields and reduce each Deps field to its leaf-ID list. Extend the `plan.md` §Blocked obligations entry to cover `V9l-T` alongside `V9l` as both blocked on the same clause-(a) resolution, and carry the `V9l-T` test-authoring risk statement into that section.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve the `V18c` dep edge in both leaves' Deps fields — it is the mechanical gate, not the relocated governance prose.
- Preserve the existing cross-references from both leaves to `plan.md#blocked-obligations`.
