## Finding

The *Discard-site disposition* paragraph in `runtime-event-channel.md` opens with a genuinely normative sentence — the four excluded kinds (`validation`, `context_overflow`, `cancelled`, `invoke_callee`) emit nothing on the channel even at a discard site (`let _ = @"…"`, `void`-tail discard, or any other `Err`-dropping disposition). That sentence is load-bearing: it resolves the otherwise-open question raised by the always-log set's own "regardless of whether the author matched, propagated via `?`, or discarded it via `let _ =`" rule, which a reader could mistakenly extend to the excluded kinds. It also forwards to the consumer-side counterpart owned by [Query — Observability of discarded results].

The remainder of the paragraph — "The exclusion rationale survives the discard case for each kind: for `validation` … For `cancelled` … For `context_overflow` … For `invoke_callee` …" — is decision-log rationale, not a constraint. It restates, in the discard context, the per-kind justification already given verbatim by the immediately-preceding paragraph ("`validation`, `context_overflow`, `cancelled`, and `invoke_callee` are deliberately **not** in the always-log set: …"). It constrains no implementation behaviour beyond what the opening sentence already pins, and forces a reader to parse four sentences of motivation to confirm there is no additional rule.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Retain the paragraph's first sentence (the normative discard-site emission rule, including the `let _ = @"…"` / `void`-tail enumeration) and the closing cross-reference to [Query — Observability of discarded results]. Delete the per-kind rationale span beginning "The exclusion rationale survives the discard case for each kind:" through the four per-kind sentences. The resulting paragraph states the rule and its consumer-side counterpart with no decision-log motivation. (If the per-kind motivation is judged worth preserving, move it to an ADR rather than the normative page — but the preceding exclusion paragraph already records the same per-kind reasoning, so an ADR copy is optional.)

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

No implementer diverges: the normative discard-site behaviour is fully carried by the paragraph's opening sentence and the preceding exclusion paragraph. The redundant per-kind rationale only inflates the page and repeats motivation already stated one paragraph earlier.
