## Problem

Two distinct spec-topic files sit side by side in `docs/spec_topics/`, differing only by a trailing `-and-orientation`: `overview.md` (titled "Overview and Conceptual Model", the conceptual-model document) and `overview-and-orientation.md` (titled "Overview and orientation", the implementer-facing orientation document). Both open with an identical `## Overview` heading and near-identical first sentences, so the filename suffix is the only signal distinguishing them. A maintainer following a cross-reference, or grepping for "the overview page", cannot tell from the filename alone which document carries which role and risks editing the wrong file.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `fecb504` (2026-05-04, original spec-topic split) and `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04, 100KB/30KB size-cap split), compounded by `81bf52a` (2026-06-11, `spec/` → `spec_topics/` collapse)
**History:** `fecb504` created `spec_topics/overview.md` with the title `# Overview and Conceptual Model` as part of the original monolithic-`spec.md` split, at which point that page was the only "Overview"-titled page in `spec_topics/`. `f5e89f4` then enforced the 100KB hard / 30KB recommended size cap by splitting the root `spec.md` into three section pages, one of which — `spec/overview-and-orientation.md`, titled `# Overview and orientation` — duplicated the leading "Overview and …" stem; at that point the collision existed across two directories (`spec/` vs `spec_topics/`). `81bf52a` then collapsed `spec/` into `spec_topics/` to restore the single-delegation-target architecture, content-preservingly, which co-located the two "Overview and …" pages in the same directory. No single commit authored the collision; it is the cumulative effect of the size-cap split inheriting an "Overview and orientation" label without checking it against the pre-existing `overview.md` title, followed by the directory collapse that moved them next to each other.

## Solution approach

Rename `docs/spec_topics/overview.md` to `conceptual-model.md` and retitle its H1 to "Conceptual Model". Update the REQ-ID prefix-table row in `req-id-prefix-table-active-b.md` and the Reading-order entry in `language-and-architecture.md` (both link text and target), and repoint the inbound references, including the `M-minimal-slash-command` / `M-T-minimal-slash-command` plan Spec-field citations. Grep the corpus and plan for `overview.md` anchored so the pattern does not also match `overview-and-orientation.md`, to catch stray links that would otherwise 404.
