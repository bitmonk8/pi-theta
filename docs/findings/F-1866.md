## Finding

"the runtime guards the propagation with a one-shot flag so a re-entrant `loomAbort.abort()` does not double-cancel" pins an internal mechanism where the observable contract is idempotent abort propagation, and states no observable Then-clause for the double-cancel failure (so a test has no oracle).

**Suggested fix:** Restate behaviourally: abort propagation is idempotent — a re-entrant `loomAbort.abort()` MUST invoke Pi's `abort()` exactly once and MUST NOT propagate an unhandled exception; demote the one-shot flag to a non-normative note.
