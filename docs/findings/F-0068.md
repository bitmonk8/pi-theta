## Finding

`docs/plan.md` §Spec coverage links the coverage matrix with the sentence "every executable spec REQ-ID mapped to its closing leaf(s). Empty today; populated as leaves are authored." The "Empty today; populated as leaves are authored." clause is false: `docs/plan_topics/coverage-matrix.md` is fully populated (86 table rows spanning the numbered runtime-obligation REQ-IDs and the code-keyed/governance sections), with every prefix mapped to one or more closing leaves.

A reader — or a release-gate operator — reasoning from plan.md alone would conclude no REQ-IDs are yet mapped and that the H5a closing gate / H6a live-corpus activation have nothing to audit. That directly contradicts the matrix and the §Release gate narrative, undermining trust in the coverage-gate readiness signal at exactly the document a fresh implementer opens first.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** 15f69aa — pi-loom plan: finish scaffold/template re-pivot from commit 657ee76 (2026-05-26, plan author); c6a664e — pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review (2026-06-10) … b61322f — pi-loom plan: resolve "DISC-4 split across two coverage-matrix rows" (2026-06-12)
**History:** Commit 15f69aa added the "Empty today; populated as leaves are authored." clause when `coverage-matrix.md` was a 1-row scaffold, so the clause was accurate at the time. The matrix was subsequently grown to 86 rows by a run of population commits (c6a664e on 2026-06-10 through the "resolve …" series ending b61322f on 2026-06-12), none of which updated the plan.md sentence — leaving it stale through the interaction of the introducing scaffold edit and the later population edits.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan.md` §Spec coverage, strike the clause `Empty today; populated as leaves are authored.` from the Spec-coverage-matrix bullet, leaving `- [Spec coverage matrix](./plan_topics/coverage-matrix.md) — every executable spec REQ-ID mapped to its closing leaf(s).` The remaining sentence is accurate and self-contained. The H5a closing-gate / H6a live-corpus activation are already described in How-to-use step 5 and §Release gate, so the bullet need not restate the gate; if the author prefers to add a forward pointer instead of a bare deletion, replace the struck clause with a reference to those existing sections rather than a fresh gate description.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

A reader or release-gate operator consulting plan.md would conclude the coverage matrix is empty and that no REQ-IDs are mapped, contradicting the 86-row matrix and the §Release gate / H5a / H6a machinery. No leaf is blocked and no gate misfires, but the stale claim erodes confidence in the coverage signal and can send a fixer to "populate" a matrix that is already complete.
