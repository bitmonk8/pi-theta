## Finding

`V17a`'s **Adds** legitimately records that the leaf consumes the cancellation-runtime *semantics* of `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS = 2000` (value sourced from `patch-skew-degradation.md`) and that the constant physically resides in `V9a`'s single source-of-truth pinned-constants block — the latter grounding V17a's `V9a` **Deps** edge. After that, the same sentence appends design-motivation prose about *other* leaves: that `V18c`'s build-time literal-read assertion can reach the block, that `V9g`'s session-shutdown await cap and `V9i`'s subagent-disposal bound consume the same constant. Those are facts about V9a's ownership and about V9g/V9i/V18c's own consumption — they belong to those leaves, not to V17a's Adds, and they duplicate ownership/consumption statements already carried by `V9g`, `V9i`, and the coverage matrix.

The genuinely normative payload — that the constant has a single declaration and **no leaf redeclares it** — is buried as the trailing clause of that consumer-rationale chain. An implementer scanning V17a's Adds for V17a's own obligations reads it as motivation prose and can miss the invariant.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `7ef443c` (2026-06-10, "pi-loom plan: resolve 'SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS has no declaring owner leaf'") seeded the consumer-rationale; `622d0d3` (2026-06-11, "pi-loom plan: resolve 'SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS physical home contradicts pinned-constants-block placement'") restructured it into the cited defect.
**History:** Before `7ef443c`, V17a's Adds ended at "the late-settlement discard rules" with no mention of the constant. `7ef443c` added the constant plus the consumer clause "…the one declared source that `V9g`'s session-shutdown await cap and `V9i`'s subagent-disposal bound both consume — neither leaf redeclares it." `622d0d3` then reworked V17a from constant-owner to conceptual consumer (moving physical ownership to V9a's block and adding the `V9a` Deps edge), and in doing so expanded the clause to also name `V18c`'s build-time read and relocated "no leaf redeclares it" to the tail of the lengthened rationale chain — the exact form this finding cites. `git log -S "no leaf redeclares"` and `git log -S "session-shutdown await cap"` confirm both commits as the only touch points.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/V17a-cancellation-core.md`, edit the **Adds** so it carries only V17a's own relationship to the constant: V17a consumes the cancellation-runtime semantics of `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS = 2000` (value sourced from `patch-skew-degradation.md`), and the constant physically resides in `V9a`'s pinned-constants block (this fact justifies V17a's existing `V9a` Deps edge — keep it). Strike the cross-leaf consumer prose naming `V18c`'s build-time read and `V9g`'s/`V9i`'s consumption as motivation — those leaves already state their own consumption (`V9g` Adds/Spec, `V9i` Tests `PIC-9`, `V18c` Adds), and `V9a` is the declared owner.

If the single-declaration invariant ("no leaf redeclares `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS`") is to remain stated in V17a, write it as a standalone normative sentence rather than as the trailing clause of a consumer list. The invariant's natural home is `V9a`'s Adds (the declaring owner); relocating it there instead is acceptable. Either placement is fine — do not duplicate it in both. The spec is read-only for this fix; no spec edit is required.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

The Adds reads as a cross-leaf design narrative rather than V17a's own contract; the single-declaration invariant ("no leaf redeclares it") is the only normative content in the trailing clause and is easy to miss where it sits. An implementer of V9g/V9i/V18c is not steered to that invariant from those leaves either, so the risk it guards (a redeclared duplicate of the constant) is weakly signalled. No execution-time defect.
