## Finding

`V9a` is the single source of truth for two pinned constants exported from its
pinned-constants block: `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS = 2000` (imported by `V9g`,
`V9i`, and `V17a`) and `FACTORY_PROBABLE_CAPABILITIES` (the closed five-element
factory-probable capability list, imported/reconciled by `V18a` and `V18c`).
A wrong value in either constant propagates to every importer in one edit, and
the importers consume the symbol rather than redeclaring it, so there is no
per-importer cross-check that would catch a bad value at its use site.

The value-correctness of these constants is protected by `V18c`'s build-time
literal-read assertion (and, for `FACTORY_PROBABLE_CAPABILITIES`, additionally
by `V18a`'s partition assertion and `V9a-T`'s `PIC-5` cardinality bullet). For
`SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS` in particular, `V18c`'s literal-read assertion against
the spec source (`session-shutdown-semantics.md` §`session_shutdown`
sub-step 3) is the only build-time gate tying the exported `2000` to its
normative origin.

`V9a` never states this. It does not record the blast radius of the shared
constants (which leaves break, and how, on a wrong value), nor does it identify
`V18c`'s literal-read assertion as the load-bearing guard whose removal or
weakening would silently remove the sole build-time defence — with no recovery
posture documented for that case. A maintainer editing `V18c` would have no
signal at the owning leaf that the assertion is protecting a fan-out of five
importers.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Add a short annotation to `V9a`'s **Adds** (where the two exported constants are
already described) recording two facts: (1) the shared constants' blast radius —
that `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS` is imported by `V9g`/`V9i`/`V17a` and
`FACTORY_PROBABLE_CAPABILITIES` is reconciled by `V18a`/`V18c`, so a wrong value
propagates to every importer in one edit; and (2) that `V18c`'s build-time
literal-read assertion is the load-bearing guard tying these exported values to
their spec source, and that removing or weakening it is a regression that would
leave the constants unguarded at build time. State the recovery expectation
inline (the guard must be restored, not silently dropped). Keep the existing
`V18a` partition assertion and `V9a-T` `PIC-5` bullet references intact as the
secondary guards on `FACTORY_PROBABLE_CAPABILITIES`. The spec is read-only for
this fix; the change is internal to `V9a`'s leaf prose.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

The shared constants ship without a stated blast radius or a record that
`V18c`'s literal-read assertion is the load-bearing guard. A future edit that
drops or weakens that assertion silently removes the only build-time defence
against a wrong constant value reaching all five importers, and no recovery
posture is documented for that regression; the omission does not block any leaf
from being picked up, so it is guidance-level rather than correctness-level.
