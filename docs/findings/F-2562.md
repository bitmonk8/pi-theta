## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Either rename the anchor to `overflow-token-count-extraction` and repoint every inbound link, or rename the heading to *Overflow token extraction* to match the existing anchor — choose whichever minimises inbound-link churn. Keep anchor and heading in lockstep.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

The cost is reader friction and corpus-hygiene drift — an un-glossaried load-bearing term, a self-inconsistent seam catalogue, and an open spec-author action item that lives in normative prose where the version-bump checklist and non-goals catalogue cannot find it.
