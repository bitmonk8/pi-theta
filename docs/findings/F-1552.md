## Problem

The *Runtime construction obligation* paragraph in `diagnostic-shape.md`'s `#session-shutdown-details-conventions` block states its requirement by enumerating forbidden JavaScript constructs (`...event` spread, `Object.assign(..., event, ...)`, `Object.keys(event)` copy loop) and prescribing the positive construction as "each pinned field assigned by an explicit static property name", rather than the observable property the spec needs: that the `details.event` member set on the wire is exactly the per-code set this paragraph pins (`{reason}` for `loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded`; `{reason, loom, invocation_id}` for `loom/runtime/cancelled-by-session-shutdown`) regardless of what the SDK `SessionShutdownEvent` carries. The placement compounds the issue: the rule is an emission-side construction obligation, a sibling of PIC's *Construction-site wrap* and *Hoist obligation* clauses on `#diagnostic-emission-isolation`, but homing it on the shape page splits one tightly-coupled MUST set across two files and forces a PIC emission-isolation auditor to chase a cross-reference out to a different topic page.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** commit-tracked
**Introducing commits:** `52b9101` (2026-05-11) — `pi-loom spec: resolve "T24 — Fork-reason watcher closure leaves the extension in an unspecified, silently degraded state"`
**History:** The *Runtime construction obligation* paragraph was authored in `52b9101` as part of the T24 fix, which introduced the `loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded` row and the new `details.event.{reason}` nesting convention; the paragraph was added together with the closed-`{reason}` strictness pin it defends, on what was then `docs/spec_topics/diagnostics.md`. The forbidden-construct enumeration ("no `...event` spread, no `Object.assign(..., event, ...)`, no per-key copy loop over `Object.keys(event)`") and the "explicit static property name" positive prescription were present at first appearance, not introduced by a later refactor. The paragraph was carried over to `docs/spec_topics/diagnostics/diagnostic-shape.md` unchanged in content (only the path changed) by `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04) when the 100KB / 30KB size cap split `diagnostics.md` into an index plus child pages. The misplacement therefore predates the split: the rule was authored on the shape page in `52b9101` while the sibling *Construction-site wrap* / *Hoist obligation* clauses landed on the PIC `diagnostic-emission-isolation` page, and the over-prescriptive framing is the form the rule has had since its single authoring commit.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the *Runtime construction obligation* paragraph in `diagnostic-shape.md`'s `#session-shutdown-details-conventions` block as a behavioural statement — the per-code `details.event` member set is statically fixed by this paragraph (`{reason}` for `loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded`, `{reason, loom, invocation_id}` for `loom/runtime/cancelled-by-session-shutdown`) and is not derived from the SDK-delivered `SessionShutdownEvent` — deleting the forbidden-construct enumeration and the explicit-static-property-name prescription. Move the emission-side construction obligation to PIC's `#diagnostic-emission-isolation` block as a sibling of the *Construction-site wrap* / *Hoist obligation* clauses, stated as a wire member-set equality requirement, and forward-link the shape-page paragraph to it.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve the runtime-constructed-vs-SDK-derived sibling discrimination: the closed-`{reason}` strictness entitlement and the *Runtime-constructed sibling carve-out* both key on it, so the behavioural restatement MUST NOT collapse the two field-source classes.
