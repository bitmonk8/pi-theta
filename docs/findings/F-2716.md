## Finding

`real-host-smoke-gate.md` refers to the single H4a-built test substitute (the in-process Pi session double whose fidelity contract `H7a`'s gate scores against) under several distinct compound names plus the bare noun, with no statement that they denote the same artefact:

- "in-process double" — opening "concrete pre-merge gate" sentence and the trigger-(2) "the in-process double's modelling of" clause.
- "deterministic in-process double" — the Pass/fail criterion sentence.
- "deterministic double" — the closing "`H7a`'s in-process gate keeps its exact-match goldens unchanged — against the deterministic double" sentence.
- "session-double fidelity-contract axes" — criterion (d), and "session-double fidelity contract" in the audience paragraph.
- bare "the double" — "the same fixture `.loom` `H7a` drives against the double".

A contributor scoring criterion (d) ("the four session-double fidelity-contract axes … hold") against the same paragraph's "deterministic double" / "in-process double" cannot confirm without inference that these are one artefact. The owning leaf `H4a` already fixes the canonical terms — it builds "the **session-double fidelity contract**" asserted by a self-check over "the in-process double" — so the smoke-gate note's drift is internal to that note, not a conflict with H4a.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

A contributor scoring the live smoke run must infer that criterion (d)'s "session-double fidelity-contract axes", the "deterministic double", and "the in-process double" are the same H4a artefact; the scoring still resolves, so it is a clarity gap rather than a divergence that produces a wrong result.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/real-host-smoke-gate.md`, use "in-process double" as the single name for the artefact throughout, matching the term `H4a` already uses for it:

- On first use (the "concrete pre-merge gate" sentence) introduce it with a short gloss, e.g. "the in-process double (the H4a-built in-process Pi session double whose fidelity contract `H7a`'s gate scores)", then refer to it as "the in-process double" thereafter.
- Replace "deterministic in-process double" (Pass/fail criterion sentence) and "deterministic double" (the "against the deterministic double" closing sentence) with "in-process double"; keep "deterministic" only as an adjective on what that double does (e.g. "its deterministic goldens"), not as part of the artefact's name.
- Replace the bare "the double" ("the same fixture `.loom` `H7a` drives against the double") with "the in-process double".

Leave criterion (d)'s "session-double fidelity-contract axes" and the audience paragraph's "session-double fidelity contract" as-is — "fidelity contract" / "fidelity-contract axes" names the contract, not the artefact, and matches H4a. The fix is confined to this note; do not rename the artefact in `H4a` or edit the spec.
