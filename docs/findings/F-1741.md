## Finding

The normative `mode: subagent` bullet in `docs/spec_topics/overview.md` closes with a
future-work aside: *"Surfacing it for testing, replay, or observability is a future
consideration (see `loom test` in [Future Considerations](./future-considerations.md))."*
This sentence neither describes loom 1.0 behaviour nor pins a loom 1.0 seam — it merely
announces that something is deferred and points at the deferral page.

`docs/spec_topics/future-considerations/surface-extensions.md` already owns the
deferral under "Tooling deferrals (no loom 1.0 impact)" with the bullet
*"**`loom test` command** for dry-run execution that runs a loom against a recorded
transcript or a stub model without hitting a live provider."* The Future Considerations
page is the single canonical home for deferred-feature inventory; restating the
deferral inside a normative mode-semantics bullet duplicates that ownership and
muddies what the bullet is contractually saying about subagent-mode behaviour.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit defect
**Introducing commits:** `8eea523` — *spec: fill implementation-blocking gaps* (2026-05-04)
**History:** The original `mode: subagent` bullet (pre-`8eea523`) read *"…the
intermediate transcript is private to the loom."* — no retention clause, no
future-work parenthetical. Commit `8eea523` rewrote the bullet to add both the
*"is not retained by the runtime after the loom returns"* clause **and** the
*"Surfacing it for testing, replay, or observability is a future consideration
(see `loom test` in [Future Considerations](#future-considerations))"* parenthetical
in the same edit. Subsequent commits (`fecb504` split the monolithic `spec.md` into
topic files, `f5e89f4` further split topics into sub-directories) only relocated
the prose and re-pointed the anchor from `#future-considerations` to
`./future-considerations.md`; neither touched the parenthetical's text. The defect
has been present at this site since `8eea523` and has not been edited since.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Delete the trailing sentence *"Surfacing it for testing, replay, or observability
is a future consideration (see `loom test` in [Future Considerations](./future-considerations.md))."*
from the `mode: subagent` bullet in `docs/spec_topics/overview.md`. The bullet
should end at *"…the intermediate transcript is private to the loom and is not
retained by the runtime after the loom returns."* No replacement cross-reference
is needed: `loom test` is already inventoried on
`docs/spec_topics/future-considerations/surface-extensions.md` under "Tooling
deferrals (no loom 1.0 impact)", and the overview's own forward-link to
`future-considerations.md` exists elsewhere in the page.

Note for the implementer: this edit is independent of any rework of the
*"is not retained by the runtime"* clause that the sibling finding may drive
(see Related Findings). Both edits touch the same sentence but resolve at
different clauses; perform this deletion regardless of how the retention clause
is ultimately narrowed.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

A reader of the normative subagent-mode bullet is interrupted by a forward-looking
aside that does not constrain loom 1.0 behaviour, and the `loom test` deferral
appears in two places with no single owner. No implementer is misled or blocked;
the cost is reader load and inventory drift if the two sites diverge.
