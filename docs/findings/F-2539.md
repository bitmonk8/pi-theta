## Finding

Under the GOV-8 anchor in `req-id-prefix-table-active-b.md`, the *Scope note* and the ex-GOV-14 *Editorial note* both state that GOV-8 is a bookkeeping discipline that does not substitute for the GOV-15 equivalence goal; GOV-15 itself restates this a third time. The Editorial note's closing reviewer-posture clause is the GOV-18-excluded reviewer-conduct content for which GOV-14 was retired.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** introduced-by-remediation (multiple commits)
**Introducing commits:** `5a772e3`, `7995d3c`, `2ff03c2`
**History:** Each fragment entered as a by-product of resolving an earlier spec-review finding, not at corpus inception:

- The GOV-8 editorial note was added by `5a772e3` (resolve "GOV-14 two SHOULD-NOTs admit no mechanical witness", 2026-06-05), which retired GOV-14 and demoted its reviewer-posture text to a non-normative note retained at the former site.
- The retired-table migration HTML comment was added by `7995d3c` (resolve "GOV-15 buried in retired-prefix-table graveyard file", 2026-06-05), which relocated GOV-15 to `source-language-stability.md`.
- The "rejected-candidate record (closed)" paragraph was added by `2ff03c2` (resolve "Source-language stability bullet leaves equivalence undefined…", 2026-05-10) and carried forward unchanged through the file-split commit `f5e89f4`.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Collapse the two consecutive notes into a single non-normative note at the GOV-8 site that makes the bookkeeping-vs-equivalence point once and cross-links GOV-15; drop the reviewer-posture clause ("not a defect and not a loom 1.0 correctness finding"), which duplicates GOV-15's own back-reference and reasserts GOV-18-excluded reviewer conduct. Leave the Retired REQ-IDs table's GOV-14 row in `anchor-scheme-and-retired.md` unchanged.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

No implementer or runtime observer is affected — all three fragments are non-normative. Left in place they add reviewer-reading surface and dilute the normative prose (notably the rejected-candidate decision-log inside the audit-methodology section), and the editorial note keeps GOV-18-excluded reviewer-posture text alive in the corpus.
