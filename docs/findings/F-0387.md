## Problem

`V10d`'s **Adds** field commits the leaf to the settings-re-merge sub-arm of the watcher-time reload failure-injection seam â€” routing a synthetic failure onto the `loom-system-note` surfacing path with `triggerTurn:false`, without standing up a live watcher. But `V10d`'s **Tests** and **Ships when**, and its paired `V10d-T`, assert only the 250 ms `Clock`-driven debounce coalescing; nothing in the pair exercises the settings-re-merge arm `V10d` adds. The only assertion touching the arm lives in `V4e`'s `ERR-7`, a later `V10d`-dependent leaf in another slice, and even there it is exercised only at a coarse combined-routing level. `V10d`'s **Ships when** can therefore pass green with the arm unimplemented or mis-wired (wrong channel, wrong `triggerTurn`, or live-watcher dependence), undetected until `V4e` is built.

## Solution approach

Add a **Tests** bullet to `V10d-T-reload-debounce.md` (and the matching implementation-side **Tests** bullet to `V10d-reload-debounce.md`) that feeds a synthetic settings-re-merge failure to `ReloadFailureInjector.injectReloadFailure` and asserts the arm routes onto `loom-system-note` with `triggerTurn:false` and no live watcher. Extend `V10d`'s **Ships when** to name that routing outcome alongside the existing debounce assertion, so the leaf cannot ship green without exercising the arm it adds. Coordinate the new test's `injectReloadFailure` call form with the discriminated parameter shape resolved under F-0372 rather than inventing a `V10d`-local shape.

## Solution constraints

- The settings-re-merge arm is contributed against the `ReloadFailureInjector` interface `V9b` declares (the single declaration site); do not declare a `V10d`-local interface or pin a `V10d`-local `injectReloadFailure` call shape.
- `V4e`/`ERR-7` is out of scope for this fix; whether `ERR-7` isolates the arm as a distinct assertion is a separate `V4e` concern, not a reason to leave `V10d` ungated.
