## Finding

The `V2a` leaf ("Type grammar and loom literal sublanguage") delivers three things in one task: the type-expression parser (primitive / named / generic `array`/`Result` / inline-object / union / literal types, return-only `void`), the loom literal sublanguage (primitive / named-value / array / object literals with full-field and is-literal checks), and `array<T>` type-sink resolution. Its `Tests` split cleanly along that seam â€” `generic-arity-mismatch`, `void-in-non-return-position`, `result-in-schema-position`, and `array-no-common-type` exercise the type parser and array sink; `default-not-literal`, `tool-arg-not-literal`, and `missing-object-field` exercise the literal sublanguage.

These two halves have disjoint downstream consumers. Every leaf that declares `V2a` in its `Deps` â€” `V2b`, `V2c`, `V2d`, `V2e`, `V5a`, `V11d` â€” consumes the type-expression parser; none of them consumes the literal sublanguage. The leaves that exercise literal positions (`default:` literals, tool-arg literals, object literals) are `V6b` and `V14a`, neither of which declares `V2a` in its `Deps` at all. Because the two concerns share only the `array<T>` sink resolution (array literals resolve their element type through it), the coupling is real but not mandatory: the type parser can ship and unblock the entire `V2`/`V5` type-consuming front while the literal sublanguage is still in progress.

Bundling them forces the type-parser consumers to wait on literal-sublanguage work that none of them needs before `V2a` can tag complete, and rolls two independently red-testable parsers into a single acceptance gate.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

The bundled leaf still yields a working implementation, so nothing diverges or ships uncovered. The cost is sequencing: the six type-parser consumers (`V2b`/`V2c`/`V2d`/`V2e`/`V5a`/`V11d`) cannot pick up until the unrelated literal sublanguage is also complete, serializing the early V2/V5 build front behind work none of them consume, and the two parsers share one red-test acceptance gate instead of two.

**Shape:** single
