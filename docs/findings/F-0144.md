## Problem

`V8c` and its paired tests leaf `V8c-T` each implement exactly `PIC-11` (the `SchemaValidator` seam), but their **Spec.** fields cite `host-interfaces-core.md` plus `implementation-notes.md`. The normative `PIC-11` contract ‚Äî one-pass multi-error reporting, no coercion/defaulting, in-doc `$ref`, silent unknown `format`, determinism, per-runtime instancing, and slug-cache byte-verify with `validator-cache-collision` ‚Äî lives in `host-interfaces-services.md` (anchor `#pic-11`), which neither leaf cites. `host-interfaces-core.md` contains no `PIC-11` / `SchemaValidator` / `slug-cache` content. An implementer who reads only the cited spec pages never opens the page defining the obligation the leaf exists to close.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** c6a664e (`pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review`)
**History:** The `SchemaValidator` seam was first authored in c6a664e bundled inside `V8a-checkpoint-validator-seams.md` (then titled "`Checkpoint` and `SchemaValidator` seams"), whose **Spec.** field already cited `host-interfaces-core.md` + `implementation-notes.md` and omitted `host-interfaces-services.md` ‚Äî so both PIC-10 and PIC-11 were mis-cited from the corpus's first plan build. The over-bundling split (cb8737a, `resolve "Systemic leaf over-bundling across the plan corpus"`) carved out `V8c`/`V8c-T` as new files and copied the wrong `host-interfaces-core.md` citation forward verbatim; it propagated rather than introduced the defect.

## Solution approach

In both `docs/plan_topics/V8c-schema-validator-seam.md` and `docs/plan_topics/V8c-T-schema-validator-seam.md`, change the **Spec.** field's first citation from `host-interfaces-core.md` to `host-interfaces-services.md`, retaining the `implementation-notes.md` citation. This matches the `core` + `services` citation pattern already used by the sibling seam leaves `V8b` and `V8d`.

## Solution constraints

- The spec is read-only for this fix ‚Äî only the two plan leaf files change.
- PIC-10's parallel mis-citation in the `V8a`/`V8a-T` leaves is out of scope (owned by its own finding).
