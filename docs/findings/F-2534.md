## Finding

The "Surface extensions without a dedicated topic-page seam" preamble exists to justify why its five items are excluded from the Scope-page "Forward-compatibility seams" tally (GOV-31 accounting), which is bookkeeping for a count owned elsewhere, not a forward-compatibility obligation.

**Spec edits:** In `surface-extensions.md`, remove the multi-sentence preamble paragraph that explains the tally exclusion and per-item carrier rationale, and the parallel exclusion sentence in the "gated on an open design decision" sub-section. Retain at most a one-line note that items in these sub-buckets are not inventoried seams and are therefore excluded from the tally per GOV-31; let the canonical tally definition on the Scope page and GOV-31 own the accounting.

**Relationship:** same-cluster — resolves independently in `surface-extensions.md`; co-resolves the duplicate exclusion sentence in the sibling "gated on an open design decision" sub-section.
