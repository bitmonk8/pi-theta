## Finding

Output (c) pins *green* to "each of the six surfaces above exercised", but provides no mechanism that fails `npm test` when the contributor-chosen `.loom` omits a surface, so green is necessary-but-not-sufficient for the stated predicate and a bump skipping a surface lands undetected.

The step-6 anti-regression clause — "do not silently regress a provider for which pi-ai has added seed support" — was appended as a bare imperative without a normative modal in 52219c4, and has carried that undefined weight through the 2026-06-04 size-cap split and the 2026-06-08 `spec/`→`spec_topics/` collapse since. The output-(c) runtime-evidence gate first appeared in 81ab342 (2026-06-10) as a vaguer "representative integrated `.loom`"; ff288ce then sharpened it into the "each of the six surfaces above exercised" green predicate and added the `H4a` cross-reference, but pinned no per-surface coverage assertion — so the green-proxy weakness this finding names was refined into its present form by ff288ce while leaving the contributor-chosen `.loom` unguarded.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In the output-(c) sentence of `version-bump-triggers.md`, replace the bare "with each of the six surfaces above exercised" green predicate with a requirement that the six-surface coverage be **mechanically enforced**: either (i) require the gate's fixture to carry one coverage marker/assertion per surface so the run reddens when any of the six is unexercised, or (ii) cross-reference an explicit `H4a` guarantee that the end-to-end harness fails when a named surface is absent from the driven `.loom`. State that contributor selection of the `.loom` does not substitute for this mechanical check. If the cross-reference form is chosen, ensure the `H4a` harness contract (`docs/plan_topics/H4a-factory-shell-and-harness.md`) and `V18d`'s acceptance test actually own a per-surface coverage assertion rather than only the existing fidelity-contract self-check.

## Consequence

**Severity:** correctness

Two reasonable implementers diverge on whether the runtime-evidence gate enforces six-surface coverage: one builds `H4a`/`V18d` to redden when a surface is absent, the other lets a green run satisfy the gate regardless of which surfaces the chosen `.loom` drives. In the latter case a Pi minor that regresses behaviour behind an unexercised surface lands at the new pin with `npm test` green, defeating output (c)'s purpose. The undefined modal on step 6 leaves the anti-regression obligation's conformance weight unverifiable.
