## Finding

`conventions.md` §Leaf format defines **Deps.** as "Other leaf IDs that must be complete first. Listed `-` if none." — a leaf-ID list whose entries drive the pickability predicate. The leaf-template carries no Notes field; its only sanctioned content below the Deps line is the authoring-guidance parenthetical the template instructs the author to delete.

`H6a` (`docs/plan_topics/H6a-live-corpus-activation.md`) keeps a multi-paragraph italic parenthetical immediately below its `**Deps.** H5b, H5c, H7a` line. The block carries dependency-selection rationale — why the `H5c`/`H7a` edges are pinned directly on `H6a` rather than threaded transitively through `H5b`, the assertion that `H5b` owns the complete coverage-producing set, and the drift-cannot-occur argument. This is leaf prose, not part of the leaf-ID Deps field the format defines, and `H6a` is one of only two leaves (`H5b` being the other) that attach such a block.

The leaf-ID list on the `**Deps.**` line itself is clean (`H5b, H5c, H7a`), so the pickability machinery is unaffected; the deviation is purely one of organisation — content placed in the field-region the format reserves for the dependency list.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/H6a-live-corpus-activation.md`, the `**Deps.**` field must carry only the leaf-ID list (`H5b, H5c, H7a`) as §Leaf format defines. Relocate the dependency-selection rationale currently in the italic parenthetical below the Deps line — the explanation of why the `H5c`/`H7a` edges are pinned directly on `H6a` rather than inherited transitively through `H5b`, and the warn-only/hard-fail "cannot drift" argument — out of the Deps field, preserving the content as leaf prose. The leaf's **Adds.** narrative already carries `H6a`'s sequencing rationale (it sequences immediately after `H5b`, must follow every coverage-producing leaf, etc.) and is the existing descriptive field that field-format already sanctions for such prose; the exact arrangement is the implementer's to choose. Do not drop the rationale content — the direct-pin decision for `H5c`/`H7a` is non-obvious and worth retaining.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

The `**Deps.**` leaf-ID list (`H5b, H5c, H7a`) is correct and the dep-driven pickability predicate operates on it unaffected; the multi-paragraph rationale block below it deviates from §Leaf format (which defines Deps as a leaf-ID list and provides no Notes field) but misleads no implementer and blocks no leaf. Pure organisation.
