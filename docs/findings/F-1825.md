## Finding

BNDR-3 imposes a MUST on the failure-mode templates table (in determinism-cancellation-failure.md) but is placed with BNDR-1/BNDR-2 in the envelope file; a reader of the templates file sees the two row prefixes without the MUST forbidding their collapse.

**Suggested fix:** Co-locate BNDR-3 with `### Failure-mode templates (normative)` in determinism-cancellation-failure.md.
