## Problem

`V15a` directly consumes `FileSystem.realpath` — its **Adds**, `INV-1`, and **Ships when** all turn on `realpath` discovery-root containment — but its **Deps** (`V15a-T, V10a, V2b, V3d, V8a, V5e`) omit `V8b`, the leaf that exclusively owns the `realpath` member of the `FileSystem` seam. `V15a-T` carries the same gap (`Deps. V10a, V2b, V3d, V8a, V5e`). Under the `conventions.md` Class-2 seam rule a member binds only when a leaf listing the owner in `Deps` names that member; `realpath` is the one `FileSystem` member with no Deps-naming binder, leaving `V15a`'s direct dependency on `V8b` undeclared and the seam member non-binding.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** c6a664e (2026-06-10) — "pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review"
**History:** `git log --diff-filter=A --follow` shows both `V15a-invocation-core.md` and `V15a-T-invocation-core.md` were created in c6a664e. `git show c6a664e:…V15a-invocation-core.md` confirms the created file already used `realpath` in **Adds** and `INV-1` while its **Deps** read `V15a-T, V10a, V2b, V3d` — no `V8b`. `git log -G 'V8b' -- …V15a-invocation-core.md` and the same over `V15a-T` return no commits, so `V8b` has never appeared in either leaf's text. The realpath-without-`V8b` gap dates to the leaf pair's first authored version.

## Solution approach

Add `V8b` to the **Deps** field of both `docs/plan_topics/V15a-invocation-core.md` and `docs/plan_topics/V15a-T-invocation-core.md`, so each leaf that names `realpath` in its `Adds`/`Tests` also lists the seam owner in `Deps`. This binds the `realpath` seam member under the `conventions.md` Class-2 rule and records `V15a`'s direct dependency edge on `V8b`.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the parallel undeclared-seam `Deps` edits owned by the F-0098 siblings (V15c/V15c-T, V10b/V10b-T, V10c/V10c-T, V11b/V11b-T, and the C10 / V12a edit) — touch only `V15a` and `V15a-T`.
- The spec is read-only for this fix; `realpath` is spec-owned and already declared in `V8b`'s **Adds**.
