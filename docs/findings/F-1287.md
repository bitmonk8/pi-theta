## Problem

The Trigger cell of `loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded` in `docs/spec_topics/diagnostics/code-registry-host.md` restates the entire degraded-state state machine that PIC's `session-only-degraded-state.md` owns (along with content owned by sibling PIC pages) rather than referencing it, unlike its neighbouring registry rows which keep their Trigger cells scoped to the per-row `details` contract and link out for the rest. The cell and PIC currently agree, but the cell is a second source of truth: any future edit to PIC's degraded-state surface silently incurs an unflagged co-edit obligation on this registry row, and the existing closure-gate tooling (`DIAG-4`) covers only the Message column.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** `52b9101` (2026-05-11, "pi-loom spec: resolve \"T24 — Fork-reason watcher closure leaves the extension in an unspecified, silently degraded state\"")
**History:** `git log -S 'session-shutdown-runtime-degraded' --reverse --diff-filter=A` over the spec corpus identifies `52b9101` as the commit that simultaneously authored the PIC `reason: "new" | "resume" | "fork"` edge-case bullet (then resident in `docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract.md`) and the `loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded` registry row (then resident in `docs/spec_topics/diagnostics.md`). The row's Trigger cell at that commit already restated the PIC state-machine content rather than referencing it; the duplication is an authoring-time choice, not a later mirror that fell out of sync. Subsequent commits added refinements that PIC and the row co-received: `aa2f9d7` (`String(event.reason)` coercion-throw fallback, sibling rows) and `bf85092` (non-`Error`-throw coercion, `session-shutdown-teardown-step-failed`) touched neighbour rows; the file-split commit `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04, "enforce 100KB hard / 30KB recommended size cap on spec set") moved the row verbatim from `diagnostics.md` into the new `diagnostics/code-registry-host.md` child and rewrote PIC cross-link paths under the new `pi-integration-contract/` subdirectory, but added no Trigger-cell content. No commit after `52b9101` has trimmed the cell or introduced a GOV-30-style lock-step obligation between the row and PIC.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the `loom/host/session-shutdown-runtime-degraded` Trigger cell so it retains only what the registry table is authoritative for — the minimal emission condition, the `details` shape, and the `console.error` routing rationale — and forward-links to PIC's `session-only-degraded-state.md#session-only-reason-degraded-state` for the state-machine narrative, the diagnostic-emission predicate, the emission seam, the co-emission rules, and the corner cases. Delete the restated state-machine content (the transition narrative, the slash-arm note templates, the short-circuit predicate, the re-entry corner cases, the co-emission narratives, and the `capturedEventReason`-vs-`shutdownReason` discussion) from the cell.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve the existing anchors `runtime-degraded-emission-condition` and `runtime-degraded-co-emission` verbatim; broader-spec inbound cross-references resolve to them.
- Out of scope: the Message column (the `DIAG-4` wire-contract surface) and the sibling `session-shutdown-teardown-step-failed` row's per-call `details.call` enumeration (canonical there, not duplicated elsewhere).
