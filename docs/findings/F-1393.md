## Problem

The §*Pi-side slash-handler promise lifecycle (consumption posture)*
paragraph (anchor `#pi-slash-handler-promise-lifecycle-presupposition`)
on `host-interfaces-core.md` enumerates six "the loom presupposes Pi …"
behavioural presuppositions (i)–(vi), embedded mid-paragraph under
**Cancellation source**. Every other named loom-side presupposition of
this kind lives on `host-prerequisites.md`, the page a reviewer or
version-bump contributor consults to enumerate loom's load-bearing
assumptions about Pi. Because these six sit on the
type-and-cancellation-surface page instead, a presupposition-inventory
reviewer reading only `host-prerequisites.md` will not discover them, and
a future Pi minor that breaks any of the six can silently degrade or hang
the cancellation path without surfacing against any named prerequisite.
The defect is placement, not content.

**History:** Commit `e89e42e` introduced the *Pi-side slash-handler promise lifecycle (consumption posture)* paragraph with the first three "loom presupposes …" clauses (i)–(iii), placing it under §**Cancellation source** in the then-monolithic `pi-integration-contract.md` rather than under the host-prerequisites section. `aebec4e` (item (g)), `7f07a96` (presupposition (v)), and `fdc5115` (presupposition (vi)) successively grew the paragraph to four, five, and six presuppositions while preserving its location. `f5e89f4` then split the monolithic file into `spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/*`, carrying the paragraph forward onto `host-interfaces-core.md` (`#pi-slash-handler-promise-lifecycle-presupposition`) and the named-presuppositions onto the sibling `host-prerequisites.md`, fixing the divergent placement in its post-split shape without correcting it. The defect is the cumulative product of the original siting choice and the four follow-on growth/split commits that each preserved it.

## Solution approach

Move the *Pi-side slash-handler promise lifecycle (consumption posture)*
paragraph — preamble, the six (i)–(vi) presuppositions, and its closing
sentence — from `host-interfaces-core.md` to `host-prerequisites.md`
alongside the existing named presuppositions, preserving the
`id="pi-slash-handler-promise-lifecycle-presupposition"` anchor verbatim
at the relocated paragraph. Leave a short pointer under
**Cancellation source** forward-linking the relocated paragraph so a
reader of the runtime-side cancellation chain still reaches the
consumption posture. Repoint the four version-bump checklist links
((g), (j), (v), (ad)), the `cancellation.md` §*Granularity*
cross-reference, and the `host-interfaces-services.md` `Checkpoint` seam
cross-reference from
`host-interfaces-core.md#pi-slash-handler-promise-lifecycle-presupposition`
to `host-prerequisites.md#pi-slash-handler-promise-lifecycle-presupposition`.
Widen the **Spec** headers of plan leaves `V17a`, `V17b`, `V17c`, and
`V18c` to add `host-prerequisites.md`.

## Solution constraints

- The stub left under **Cancellation source** MUST forward-link the relocated paragraph, not duplicate the six presuppositions — duplicating them re-creates the inventory-divergence the move removes.
