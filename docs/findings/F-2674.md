## Finding

Step 6's "do not silently regress a provider for which pi-ai has added seed support" is the substantive anti-regression obligation of the step yet carries no normative modal, leaving its conformance weight undefined.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** 52219c4 — Address all 41 spec-review findings (2026-05-06, Thomas Andersen); ff288ce — pi-loom spec-plan: fix F-1735+F-2265 — operationalize version-bump output (c) gate (harness invocation, six-surface fixture, green predicate) (2026-06-27, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The two halves entered independently (not as a causal interaction). The step-6 anti-regression clause — "do not silently regress a provider for which pi-ai has added seed support" — was appended as a bare imperative without a normative modal in 52219c4, and has carried that undefined weight through the 2026-06-04 size-cap split and the 2026-06-08 `spec/`→`spec_topics/` collapse since. The output-(c) runtime-evidence gate first appeared in 81ab342 (2026-06-10) as a vaguer "representative integrated `.loom`"; ff288ce then sharpened it into the "each of the six surfaces above exercised" green predicate and added the `H4a` cross-reference, but pinned no per-surface coverage assertion — so the green-proxy weakness this finding names was refined into its present form by ff288ce while leaving the contributor-chosen `.loom` unguarded.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Restate the trailing clause of step 6 with an explicit modal, e.g. "the contributor MUST NOT default a provider to `omitted` when pi-ai documents a seed field for it" (or the SHOULD-level equivalent if the obligation is intended as advisory), so the requirement's weight is unambiguous and consistent with the "MUST consult …" obligation it sits beside.

## Consequence

**Severity:** correctness

Two reasonable implementers diverge on whether the runtime-evidence gate enforces six-surface coverage: one builds `H4a`/`V18d` to redden when a surface is absent, the other lets a green run satisfy the gate regardless of which surfaces the chosen `.loom` drives. In the latter case a Pi minor that regresses behaviour behind an unexercised surface lands at the new pin with `npm test` green, defeating output (c)'s purpose. The undefined modal on step 6 leaves the anti-regression obligation's conformance weight unverifiable.
