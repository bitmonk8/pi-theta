## Problem

The `V5d-T` tests-leaf H1 heading reads `# \`V5d-T\` — Schema-subset gate, lowering, and canonical hash (tests)`, claiming the schema **lowering** and **canonical hash** scopes that the leaf does not own — `V5d`'s Adds explicitly disowns both, and that work lives in `V5f` / `V5f-T`. The heading also drops the `reject` qualifier that the paired `V5d` leaf and the plan.md V5 toc entry both use (`Schema-subset reject gate`), and it collides with `V5f-T`'s heading (`Schema lowering and canonical hash (tests)`), so both test leaves appear to cover lowering and the canonical hash. A prior leaf-split renamed `V5d` and the toc to `reject gate` but left the `V5d-T` partner heading at the pre-split title.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commit:** `cb8737a` — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"Systemic leaf over-bundling across the plan corpus\"" (2026-06-12)
**History:** The title string `Schema-subset gate, lowering, and canonical hash` was present at the corpus's first commit `c6a664e` (2026-06-10) across the `V5d` heading, the `V5d-T` heading, and the plan.md toc — where it was *accurate*, because `V5d` then owned the full gate + lowering + canonical-hash scope. `cb8737a` split lowering and the canonical hash out into the new `V5f` / `V5f-T` leaves and renamed `V5d`'s heading and the plan.md toc entry to `Schema-subset reject gate`, but did not touch `docs/plan_topics/V5d-T-subset-lowering.md` (its stat names only `V5d`, `V5f`, and `V5f-T`). The unchanged `V5d-T` heading thereby became an over-claim at that split.

## Solution approach

Rename the `V5d-T` H1 heading in `docs/plan_topics/V5d-T-subset-lowering.md` to `# \`V5d-T\` — Schema-subset reject gate (tests)`, matching the paired `V5d` leaf heading and the plan.md V5 toc entry. This restores the `reject` qualifier and removes the collision with `V5f-T`'s heading.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the disowned lowering/hash Tests bullets in `V5d-T` (slug-collision, canonical-hash recipe, per-`$defs` sidecar) — owned by the same-cluster Tests-bullet finding, not this heading rename.

- "V5d / V5d-T filename slug `subset-lowering` conflicts with the reject-gate scope" — same-cluster (same leaf pair, naming; resolves independently via a filename rename)
