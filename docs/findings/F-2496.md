## Finding

The `| null` branch on `last_tool_name` has no loom-1.0-reachable case and is documented only as an inline schema comment, whereas the page's comparable forward-compat openness (discriminator type-openness) is a formal `loom 1.0 seam` blockquote with REQ-ID anchors.

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** f5e89f4 — docs: enforce 100KB hard / 30KB recommended size cap on spec set (2026-06-04, Thomas Andersen); 11cabfe — pi-loom spec-plan: fix F-0715 — relocate three non-DI-seam blocks out of host-interfaces-services.md + repoint cross-refs (2026-06-22, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** Items (a), (b), (d), (e), and `version-bump-intro.md`'s "three requires-re-validating sentences" enumeration all entered in f5e89f4 (2026-06-04), the commit that split the monolithic spec into topic files — the `# Queryerror` H1, the `### Query-time variants` grouping, the inline-only user-defined-error-types non-goal, and the `last_tool_name | null` comment were created in that form and never corrected. Item (f)'s mismatch is the interaction: the "three sentences" enumeration was authored in f5e89f4 to count three, and later 11cabfe (2026-06-22) added a fourth "MUST trigger a re-validation" sentence in `discovery-sources.md` without updating that count. The discovery and errors-and-results Non-goals sections (c, d) have been absent since the corpus's topic-split inception.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Either promote the `| null` branch to a `loom 1.0 seam` blockquote (with a REQ-ID anchor, in the ERR-15 style) that records why the branch is unreachable in 1.0 and what future case it reserves, or remove the `| null` and narrow `last_tool_name` to `string` if the forward-compat case is not worth reserving. Pick one; do not leave it as a bare inline comment.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

No observer behaviour diverges and no implementer is blocked. The cost is discoverability and maintenance: the mis-cased H1 and mis-grouped variant mislead readers about the canonical names; the missing Non-goals sections scatter deferred-surface boundaries so a reader cannot confirm what is intentionally out of scope; the undercounted version-bump enumeration means a future Pi bump can silently miss the discovery re-validation step.
