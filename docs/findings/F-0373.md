## Problem

`V9b` carries three conceptually independent deliverables under one leaf and one `Ships when` gate: core extension registration (steps 1–5 plus the structural-change `loom-system-note` watcher), the `ReloadFailureInjector` reload-failure seam, and the model-reference-matcher production wiring (constructing `findExactModelReferenceMatch` from `ctx.modelRegistry.getAvailable()` at the load pass and injecting it into the seam `V6a` defines). The matcher concern is orthogonal to the other two — it is model resolution, not command registration, and is witnessed by its own instance-identity assertion that does not exercise the registry-swap or watcher-note assertions. The bundling also forces a `Deps: V6a` edge onto `V9b` that exists solely to inject the matcher, coupling an early-sequenced registration leaf to a frontmatter leaf for a reason unrelated to registration or reload.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit

**Introducing commit:** `5953b185d1166ea5d7854ca2816bdc94eef380ff` — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"V6a model-reference-matcher seam has no declared producer\"" (2026-06-11)

**History:** `V9b` was created at the initial plan build (`c6a664e1194227df0702609667f3ee8be2e8c6b2`, 2026-06-10) owning registration steps 1–5, the drain-state contract, and the watcher-time reload failure-injection seam — no model-resolution concern. The orthogonal model-reference-matcher production wiring was grafted onto `V9b` by `5953b18` while closing the "V6a model-reference-matcher seam has no declared producer" finding: that commit added the "The **model-reference-matcher production wiring point** is also owned here…" Adds sentence and introduced the `V6a` Deps edge (`git show 5953b18` shows both additions to the then-named `V9b-registration-drain-state.md`), assigning `V9b` as `V6a`'s declared producer rather than a dedicated leaf. Later commits reworded the wiring (`ccedadf` added the instance-identity test, `3424d54` reconciled the witness against `V11a`) and renamed the file (`44a96f5`), but none separated the matcher concern from registration. The bundling originates at `5953b18`.

## Solution approach

Split the model-reference-matcher production wiring out of `V9b` into a thin sibling vertical leaf with its paired `-T`, leaving registration and the reload-failure seam in `V9b`. Move the matcher Adds sentence, the matcher tail of `V9b`'s `Ships when`, and the matcher-only `V6a` Deps edge to the new leaf, and move the model-reference-matcher Tests bullet from `V9b-T` to the new `-T`; the new leaf depends on `V9b` and `V6a`. Retarget the producer references in `V6a` (Adds) and `V11a` (Adds, Tests, `Ships when`, and the matcher Deps edge) from `V9b` to the new leaf, and add the new leaf to the `docs/plan.md` §Vertical slices V9 list, updating the V9/V11 interleave note if the matcher-bearing predecessor of `V11a` shifts.

## Solution constraints

- Allocate a real leaf ID for the new leaf per the `V<N><letter>` scheme in `## Plan rules`; do not leave a placeholder, and update every retargeted reference to it.
- `loom/load/model-unresolved` coverage stays owned by `V6a` — the new leaf is not a coverage-matrix REQ-ID closer and adds no coverage-matrix row.
- The spec is read-only for this fix; the matcher contract anchors are cited, not edited.
