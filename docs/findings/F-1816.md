## Finding

The `path` JSON Pointer (normative, consumer-visible) does not state whether it points at the over-limit (depth-6) node or its depth-5 parent; two implementations could emit different `path` values.

**Suggested fix:** State whether `path` points at the over-limit node or its parent, and confirm pre-order DFS over decode-order keys fixes "first".
