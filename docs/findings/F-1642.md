## Problem

The **Trust boundary** bullet under *Non-functional requirements* in `docs/spec_topics/overview-and-orientation.md` is declared *informative orientation* by the section preamble and by GOV-30, but it runs roughly 700 words and reproduces the substance of PIC-52 (`pi-integration-contract/trust-boundary.md#no-extra-mediation`) inline — the no-additional-access-channels prohibition, the `tools:` callable-set framing, the no-privilege-classification rule, the `bash`/`read` editorial commentary, the denial-surface rule, and an SDK surface-inventory presupposition sub-paragraph. An implementer reading only this bullet can build a conforming runtime without ever visiting `trust-boundary.md`, which is the failure mode the *informative orientation* declaration exists to rule out. The duplication also creates a dual-authority surface: PIC-52 and the bullet must be edited in lock-step under GOV-30, but GOV-30 reserves no mechanical witness for semantic drift between them, so any future PIC-52 amendment risks silently leaving the orientation bullet stale. Sibling bullets show the intended shape — **Runtime observability** is a tight three-sentence pointer to its channel owners.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the Trust boundary bullet into an orientation pointer of the same weight as the sibling **Runtime observability** bullet: keep the opening privilege-model kernel, then forward-link the *No additional access channels* owner at `pi-integration-contract/trust-boundary.md#no-extra-mediation` and the non-goal at `future-considerations/model-changes-and-non-goals.md#loom-1-0-non-goals`, deleting the inline restatements of the access-channel prohibition, the `tools:` callable-set framing, the privilege-classification clause, the `bash`/`read` commentary, and the denial-surface rule. Delete the SDK surface-inventory presupposition sub-paragraph from the bullet, relying on `trust-boundary.md` to carry that presupposition; if `trust-boundary.md` does not already state the absence-of-per-extension-privilege-scoping presupposition and route new-facet detection to `version-bump-step2.md#bump-checklist-privilege-scoping-facet`, migrate that one sentence there before deleting it here.
