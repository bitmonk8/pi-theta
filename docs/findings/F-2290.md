## Finding

`V17a`'s **Adds** describes the constant clause as "…the cancellation-runtime *semantics* of `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS = 2000` (value sourced from `session-shutdown-semantics.md`) **as a conceptual consumer**." The trailing qualifier "as a conceptual consumer" carries no implementer-actionable content: the surrounding sentence already states that the constant physically resides in `V9a`'s pinned-constants block and that `V17a` consumes its semantics. "Conceptual consumer" is editorial jargon that neither pins a behaviour, names a seam, nor constrains an assertion.

The phrase is pure wording; it does not change what `V17a` must implement or test.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** 622d0d3 — pi-loom plan: resolve "SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS physical home contradicts pinned-constants-block placement" (2026-06-11, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The phrase did not exist before 622d0d3. That commit relocated `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS` to `V9a`'s pinned-constants block and rewrote `V17a`'s Adds from "the single loom-owned cancellation-runtime constant `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS = 2000`" to "the cancellation-runtime *semantics* of `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS = 2000` … as a conceptual consumer", introducing the vague qualifier as part of the consumer-reframing.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/V17a-cancellation-core.md`, **Adds**, strike the trailing " as a conceptual consumer" so the clause ends after the parenthetical value-source citation: "…and the cancellation-runtime *semantics* of `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS = 2000` (value sourced from `session-shutdown-semantics.md`)." Leave the following sentence (the `V9a` physical-residence statement) untouched.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

An implementer reading the `V17a` Adds gains nothing from "as a conceptual consumer"; it is filler that does not affect implementation or acceptance. Nothing breaks if it ships, but it dilutes the Adds clause.
