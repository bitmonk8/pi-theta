## Problem

`V11f`/`V11f-T` bundle two units that draw on different spec surfaces, fixtures, and obligation families. Unit 1 — the binder per-class retry budget (`HC3-a`…`HC3-e`), the four-class failure taxonomy with its six verbatim failure-mode templates, and the depth-walk fast-fail `<ajv-summary>` rendering — is statically exercisable against the binder envelope. Unit 2 — in-flight binder-call cancellation forwarding (landing an abort during the live provider call through the `Checkpoint` seam, the sole reason `V11f` carries `V17a` in its `Deps`) — needs scripted in-flight timing and the `V17a` abort plumbing. A reviewer cannot confirm the retry caps, the templates, the depth-walk rendering, and the in-flight abort behaviour in a single pass.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commit:** `c6a664e` (2026-06-10, "pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review")
**History:** The leaf was authored with the two-concern bundle already in place — `c6a664e`'s first version of `V11f-binder-retry-taxonomy.md` titled the leaf "Binder cancellation, per-class retry budget, and failure taxonomy" and its Adds already named "the cancellation forwarding into the binder call" alongside the retry budget and taxonomy. Commit `d5dd4de` (2026-06-10, "resolve 'binder-call cancellation forwarding never asserted'") later deepened the cancellation half by adding the explicit in-flight-abort Tests bullet (with the `Checkpoint`-seam setup and the budgeted-retry sub-case) and the matching Ships-when clause; this sharpened the too-large condition by making the cancel half a concrete, separately-fixtured deliverable, but it did not introduce the bundle — the two units coexisted from the leaf's first commit.

## Solution approach

Split the in-flight binder-call cancellation-forwarding unit out of `V11f` into a new `V11` vertical-slice leaf with its `-T` partner, leaving `V11f`/`V11f-T` scoped to the retry budget, four-class taxonomy, six templates, and depth-walk fast-fail rendering. Narrow `V11f`/`V11f-T` to match — remove the cancellation-forwarding Tests bullet and its Ships-when clause, drop `V17a` from `Deps`, and strike the cancellation framing from the title and Adds. Have the new leaf depend on `V11f` (its retry machinery is the baseline for the budgeted-retry abort case) plus `V17a`. Register the new leaf in `docs/plan.md`'s `V11` slice list, in `coverage-matrix.md` (closing the cancelled-binder arm), and on the dependent `Deps` edges required by the plan's transitive-completeness conventions.
