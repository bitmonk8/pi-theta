## Problem

`SNK-a … SNK-k` are the eleven per-`kind` system-note rendering rows under `SLSH-4` in `spec_topics/slash-invocation.md`, registered as a GOV-16 stable-inline-label family. `conventions.md` §REQ-ID discipline requires every such family to close in one of two designated shapes — per-label single-leaf closure (shape (a), as `HC3-a … HC3-e` close on `V11f`) or distributed-seam closure (shape (b), as `NOCEIL-1 … NOCEIL-4` do) — with the `coverage-matrix.md` closure rationale owning the shape assignment. `SNK` receives neither: the closure rationale names `HC3`/`NOCEIL` (and `CIO`/`CEIL`) but never `SNK`, and `V12b`'s *Tests* (with its `V12b-T` partner) collapse all eleven per-`kind` rows into one bundled `SLSH-4` bullet (`the per-kind note templates (SNK-a..k) render verbatim …`) rather than the per-label bullets shape (a) requires. Because inline-label families have no mechanical closing gate, nothing verifies each per-`kind` rendering carries its own citing test, and the `SNK-k` catch-all totality property over the `QueryError` union can go unverified.

## Solution approach

Close `SNK` under per-label single-leaf closure (shape (a)), mirroring `HC3` on `V11f`, since all of `SNK-a … SNK-k` close in the single slash-dispatch-boundary leaf `V12b`. In the `V12b-top-level-err-chain.md` and `V12b-T-top-level-err-chain.md` *Tests*, replace the bundled `SLSH-4` `(SNK-a..k)` parenthetical with one *Tests* bullet per inline label, each label cited inline as `V11f` cites `HC3-a … HC3-e`, including a bullet for the `SNK-k` catch-all asserting the renderer is total over any unlisted `kind`. Extend the `coverage-matrix.md` `CIO` / `HC3` / `NOCEIL` / `CEIL` closure rationale to designate `SNK`'s closure shape as per-label single-leaf closure on `V12b`, parallel to the existing `HC3-a … HC3-e close in V11f` sentence.
