## Problem

The `H5a` leaf still carries a large set of independently-recognised closing-gate arms behind a single **Ships when**: the REQ-ID/registry/citing-test/retired-live-clash/per-prefix-numbering reconciliation arms (which share one data-source framework), the warn-only live-corpus mode, and the un-anchored normative MUST/MUST-NOT text-scan arm. That MUST text-scan arm is a separate surface — a best-effort scan over non-narrative `spec_topics/**` pages, spanning the un-enumerated-MUST, `<new>`-placeholder-MUST, and un-rowed-page sub-recognisers with their own fixtures. Because the single **Ships when** requires the gate green against the no-violation fixture and red against every seeded violation fixture at once, the leaf's `<id>-complete` pickability tag cannot fire until all arms land. This is the same over-bundling that already drove the `H5c` and `H5d` factorings.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception (compounded by later arm accretion)
**Introducing commit:** `c6a664e` (2026-06-10, "pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review") — `H5a` was authored as a multi-arm gate from birth, already bundling unmapped-REQ-ID, registry-code↔test reciprocal, retired/live clash, and per-prefix-numbering-hole arms in one leaf.
**Accretion commits:** `0603eb4` (2026-06-10, added the un-anchored-MUST arm), `953e3fa` (added the numbered-REQ-ID citing-test arm), `a593446` (un-rowed-page sub-branch), `a85a913` (`<new>`-placeholder sub-branch) each grafted a further recogniser/fixture onto the same leaf.
**Partial mitigations:** `cb8737a` (2026-06-12, "Systemic leaf over-bundling…") factored the broad-catch arm out to `H5c`; `617a3ad` (2026-06-16, "H5a bundles gate arms spanning four data sources") factored the transitive-completeness arm out to `H5d`. The arm count nonetheless stayed high because accretion outpaced the two factorings.
**History:** git-tracked; established via `git log --follow` and per-commit `git show` of the `Adds.`/`Tests.` blocks. The defect is a standing structural property of the leaf, not a regression from a single later edit, so the durable fix continues the established factoring rather than reverting any one commit.

## Solution approach

Split the un-anchored-MUST text-scan arm out of `H5a` into a new horizontal leaf, carrying its un-enumerated-MUST, `<new>`-placeholder-MUST, and un-rowed-page sub-recognisers and their seeded fixtures with it (those are facets of the one arm, not separate arms). Leave the REQ-ID/registry/citing-test/numbering/retired-live-clash reconciliation arms and the warn-only live-corpus mode in `H5a`. Author the new leaf and rescope `H5a` first so the second obligation lands on a stable baseline, then propagate the consumer references in `H5b` Deps, the `H6a` flip prose, `conventions.md`, `coverage-matrix.md`, and `docs/plan.md`.

## Solution constraints

- Leave `H5c`, `H5d`, `V7b`, and `V7b-T` Deps pointing at `H5a` — the base reconciliation machinery they depend on stays there.
- The new leaf ID follows the `H<n><letter>` plan-leaf scheme (next free letter, no reused/invented ID), per `## Plan rules`.
