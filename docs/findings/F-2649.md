## Problem

Rationale narrative, known-fragile-evidence call-outs, and governance-versioning prose are interleaved with normative prose across the `pi-integration-contract` PIC pages (notably `runtime-event-channel.md` and `unknown-reason-rule.md`). These passages restate or motivate obligations the surrounding normative text already implies. Left inline they degrade navigability without changing observable behaviour.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** d4370cb (2026-05-06, "resolve 'Consumers MUST deduplicate is a normative obligation on undefined party'" — introduced the cascade-twin/boundary-re-emission synonym pairing and the non-normative consumer-dedup framing); bf42043 (2026-06-27, F-1509 — introduced the "Group A" subset framing of the always-log set); a9fb344 (2026-06-25, F-1350 — coined PIC-45..48, including the PIC-48 anchor-stable editorial-maintenance MUSTs); f5e89f4 (2026-06-04, the 100KB/30KB size-cap split — created `unknown-reason-rule.md`/`drain-state-contract.md` as standalone pages, producing the `# Unknown reason rule` H1 vs hyphenated-term casing split and the duplicated `"degraded-needs-reload"` arm across the freshly-split pages).
**History:** The corpus is git-tracked. No single commit introduced the cluster; the residue accreted across heterogeneous edits. Several of the introducing commits were themselves prior spec-review fixes that resolved an adjacent finding but left naming/placement/scope residue behind (e.g. d4370cb fixed a normative-obligation-on-undefined-party finding while introducing the synonym pair and the consumer-facing dedup wording; bf42043/F-1509 qualified the always-log subset while solidifying the opaque "Group A/B" labels). The size-cap file split (f5e89f4) mechanically severed previously-colocated content, creating the heading-casing mismatch and the cross-page arm duplication. The defect token greps (`git log -S` over "cascade twin", "boundary re-emission", "group A", "degraded-needs-reload", "PIC-48", and `git log --follow` on the two split pages) confirm the heterogeneous, accreted entry pattern rather than a localisable single-commit origin.

## Solution approach

Delete the inline rationale narrative and known-fragile-evidence sites that the surrounding normative prose already implies, across the cited `pi-integration-contract` pages. Where a rationale is durable rather than implied, move it to an ADR/provenance appendix instead of leaving it inline in the normative sections.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the specific relocations, de-duplications, demotions, and renames owned by the co-resolve siblings F-2643, F-2644, F-2645, F-2647, F-2648, F-2650, F-2651, F-2652. This finding deletes only the residual rationale / evidence / governance-versioning narrative not claimed by those findings.
