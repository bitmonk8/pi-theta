## Finding

`V8d` owns the `Clock` and `IdSource` ambient-wrapping seams, whose entire determinism guarantee rests on no `src/**` module reaching a banned timing primitive or `crypto.randomUUID` outside its seam adapter. The `H3a` identifier-keyed architectural scan enforces this for *direct* references only; indirect forms — aliased reads (`const now = Date.now`), destructured reads, computed access (`process["env"]`), helper wrappers, and re-export indirection — are by design not mechanically detected. An undetected indirect ambient read makes the runtime non-deterministic, and no `src/**`-scoped mechanical gate witnesses it.

`conventions.md` §"No globals, statics, singletons" routes that conceded indirect-ambient residue to **two** manual-review surfaces: the *Per-phase TDD ritual* self-review step (per leaf, at implementation time) and the *Loom 1.0 release-time residue inspection* checklist item 2 (indirect ambient-primitive reads, at release time). Both `V8d` Tests bullets (`PIC-12`, `PIC-20`) describe the same residue but name **only** the self-review step — they omit the release-time residue-inspection backstop that `conventions.md` records as the second net. A reviewer or implementer reading `V8d` in isolation therefore sees a single per-leaf self-review as the sole safety net for a determinism-critical property, undercounting the coverage `conventions.md` actually specifies and leaving the two documents out of lockstep.

The underlying mechanical-detection gap is a deliberate, plan-wide risk-acceptance pattern (the same shape used for closure-captured singletons, blocking-runtime busy-waits, and broad-catch category membership) and is not itself the defect. The defect is that `V8d`'s residual-risk wording is incomplete relative to `conventions.md`: it advertises one backstop where the convention defines two.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `e94ca10` (2026-06-16, "pi-loom plan: resolve \"GOV-15 release-time reviewer inspection has no enumerated residue checklist\"")
**History:** The plan corpus is git-tracked. `V8d`'s wording "indirect forms are not mechanically detected … owned by the *Per-phase TDD ritual* self-review step" entered in `cb8737a` (2026-06-12); at that time the self-review step was the only named compensating surface, so the wording was complete. `e94ca10` (2026-06-16) then added the *Loom 1.0 release-time residue inspection* to `conventions.md`, including item 2 (indirect ambient-primitive reads) as a second backstop, but touched only `conventions.md` — `V8d` (and the parallel `H3a` note) were not updated to cross-reference the new item 2. The defect is the desync left by that single non-back-propagated edit: `conventions.md` now routes the residue to two surfaces while `V8d` still names one.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Update `V8d`'s `PIC-12` and `PIC-20` Tests bullets so the residual-risk parentheticals naming the *Per-phase TDD ritual* self-review step also cross-reference the *Loom 1.0 release-time residue inspection* checklist item 2 (indirect ambient-primitive reads) in `conventions.md` as the release-time backstop — i.e. state that indirect forms are owned by the self-review step *and*, at release time, by the residue-inspection item 2, matching `conventions.md` §"No globals, statics, singletons". The two surfaces and item 2 already exist; this is a cross-reference alignment, not a new gate.

Do not broaden the `H3a` scan to mechanically catch indirect forms: the manual-residue routing is the plan's deliberate, consistent treatment of this class, and adding mechanical detection here would diverge from the documented design.

Optional parallel touch: `H3a`'s ambient-scan Tests bullet carries the same single-backstop wording and may be aligned the same way for consistency, though the finding is scoped to `V8d`. The spec is read-only for this fix.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

Shipping `V8d` unfixed leaves a determinism-critical property documented as having a single manual backstop when `conventions.md` defines two, so a reviewer reading `V8d` alone may treat per-leaf self-review as the only net and not consult the release-time residue inspection. Implementation does not diverge — the `H3a` scan and the direct/indirect split are well-defined — so the gap is one of guidance completeness and document lockstep, not of leaf executability.
