## Problem

The same hard-ceiling-#3 budget is named two ways across the binder corpus with no statement linking them: "binder per-class retry budget" (used at the ceiling-#3 list item `#hard-ceiling-3`, `hard-ceilings.md`, and the `### Per-class retry budget (ceiling #3)` owner section with `HC3-c` / `CIO-1`) and "per-invocation retry budget" (the anchor `#per-invocation-retry-budget` on the binder topic page, plus the cross-link text from `binder-inference.md` and SM-8). The two names are orthogonal facets of one object — per slash invocation in scope, per failure-class in slot allocation — but no page says so. A reviewer reading the ceiling-#3 list sees a per-class budget and, following the cross-reference, lands on a paragraph calling itself the per-invocation retry budget with no acknowledgement that they name the same object.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** regression-by-new-feature
**Introducing commits:** `7d7bfe2` (2026-06-01, "pi-loom spec: resolve \"SM-7c/SM-8 authoritative-in-place relocation (T08/T10/T11)\"")
**History:** The "Binder per-class retry budget" wording landed in commit `be82734` (2026-05-07, "Ceiling #3 (binder retry budget): provider-failure classification rule undefined") on the ceiling-#3 list item and on `HC3-c`. The competing "per-invocation retry budget" label — together with the `<a id="per-invocation-retry-budget"></a>` anchor on the binder topic page and the SM-8 cross-link that uses that name — first appears in `7d7bfe2`, which lifted the budget paragraph to the binder topic page and authored SM-8 without normalising against the existing ceiling-#3 wording. Subsequent commits (`d0bcdb2`, `fb3474f`, and the `81bf52a` / `f5e89f4` reorganisations) preserved both spellings; the `V11f` leaf title carried the older "per-class" wording forward into the plan. No commit since `7d7bfe2` has stated the equivalence.

## Solution approach

At the canonical owner paragraph `binder/determinism-cancellation-failure.md#per-invocation-retry-budget`, add an equivalence statement that names both labels — "binder per-class retry budget" and "per-invocation retry budget" — as the same object, pinning that it is per-invocation in scope and per-class in slot allocation. At the ceiling-#3 first-use site `overview-and-orientation.md#hard-ceiling-3`, add a forward equivalence note pointing to that owner paragraph.

## Solution constraints

- Do not rename either occurrence: renaming would either invalidate the `#per-invocation-retry-budget` anchor (breaking the SM-8 / `binder-inference.md` / V11f references) or break the `HC3-a … HC3-e` per-class framing the ceiling list and `HC3-c` rely on.
- Do not coin a third synonym; the informal short form "binder retry budget" at SM-8 and the Future-Considerations bullet stays as-is.
