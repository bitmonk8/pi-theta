## Problem

The normative prohibition that none of the three transient-toast emission paths (chokidar watcher throw, unrecoverable settings I/O exception outside `loom/load/settings-*`, internal extension invariant violation) writes the `LoomRegistry` drain-state tag exists only in `diagnostics/diagnostic-shape.md`'s `#transient-toasts` paragraph. The canonical write-surface contract — `drain-state-contract.md`'s `#loom-registry-drain-state-contract` *Methods* paragraph and its *Non-normative editorial convention* — enumerates the closed four-method write surface but never names the toast paths nor restates the prohibition, and carries no reciprocal link back to the toast enumeration. An implementer auditing the tag write surface from the contract page alone therefore does not encounter the toast-paths carve-out or its consequence: a toast-surface tag write would make a later `session_shutdown` short-circuit on the `#idempotent-handler-clause` predicate and skip teardown. The rule exists in the corpus, so this is a discoverability gap rather than an outright omission.

**Introducing commits:** c2e278f — pi-loom spec-plan: fix F-1398 — pin per-arm post-failure disposition for transient toasts (2026-06-25, Thomas Andersen)

The same commit did not add a reciprocal back-reference or a restated MUST NOT to `drain-state-contract.md`, so the prohibition has lived only in `diagnostic-shape.md` since it was authored. `drain-state-contract.md`'s own *Non-normative editorial convention* ("no other writer or reader of either field is permitted") was authored separately in commit 52b9101 (2026-05-11, "T24 — Fork-reason watcher closure …") and was not revised when c2e278f introduced the toast-paths rule.

## Solution approach

Add a normative sub-paragraph to `drain-state-contract.md`'s `#loom-registry-drain-state-contract` *Methods* paragraph, after the *Non-normative editorial convention*, stating that none of the three transient-toast paths enumerated in `diagnostics/diagnostic-shape.md` `#transient-toasts` writes `drainStateTag`, and forward-cross-referencing that paragraph and the `#idempotent-handler-clause` short-circuit predicate as the consuming site. Rewrite the existing "None of the three arms writes the `LoomRegistry` drain-state tag …" sentence in `#transient-toasts` as a forward-link to the new sub-paragraph, retaining the three per-mode post-failure-state enumerations on that page.
