## Finding

The `PIC-12` and `PIC-20` Tests bullets in both `V8d-clock-id-seams.md` and its paired `V8d-T-clock-id-seams.md` carry extended parentheticals that re-explain machinery whose single authoritative home is `conventions.md` §*No globals, statics, singletons*: (i) that the same-line `// allow-ambient: <primitive> — <seam>` comment *is itself* the allow-list entry with no separate registry; (ii) that the `H3a` identifier-keyed scan enforces the spec's full WallClock ban surface (`Date.now`, `performance.now`, `Date.prototype.getTime`, `setTimeout`, `clearTimeout`); and (iii) that indirect ambient-read forms are not mechanically detected and are owned by the *Per-phase TDD ritual* self-review step. Each of these three statements is already stated verbatim, and authoritatively, in the `conventions.md` §*No globals* rule.

Roughly half of each bullet is therefore enforcement-posture prose, not a test assertion. The actual assertions are short: `PIC-12` asserts there is no *direct* ambient timing reference outside the `WallClock` adapter; `PIC-20` asserts `IdSource.newInvocationId()` is the sole *direct* `crypto.randomUUID` site. The convention-restating remainder is a second normative surface that can drift from the `conventions.md` original it duplicates.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `V8d-clock-id-seams.md` and `V8d-T-clock-id-seams.md`, reduce each of the `PIC-12` and `PIC-20` Tests bullets to the test assertion plus a cross-reference to `conventions.md` §*No globals, statics, singletons*, striking the re-explanation the convention already owns.

Strike from both bullets in both files:
- the "(that comment is itself the allow-list entry; there is no separate registry)" clauses,
- the `H3a` scan-surface enumeration "(the `H3a` identifier-keyed scan enforces the spec's full WallClock ban surface — `Date.now`, `performance.now`, `Date.prototype.getTime`, `setTimeout`, `clearTimeout` …)",
- the "indirect forms are not mechanically detected and are owned by the *Per-phase TDD ritual* self-review step" clause.

Keep the assertion content:
- `PIC-12`: that an architectural test asserts no *direct* ambient timing reference outside the `WallClock` adapter (whose `now()`→`performance.now()`, `wallNow()`→`Date.now()`, `setTimeout`/`clearTimeout` sites each carry a same-line `// allow-ambient: <primitive> — Clock` comment), with the allow-list mechanism, scan surface, and indirect-form residue referenced via `conventions.md` §*No globals, statics, singletons* rather than restated.
- `PIC-20`: that `IdSource.newInvocationId()` is the only *direct* `crypto.randomUUID` site, registered by its same-line `// allow-ambient: crypto.randomUUID — IdSource` comment, with the same cross-reference replacing the inline re-explanation.

Keep `V8d` and `V8d-T` bullets identical to each other (they are a paired tests/impl leaf and must continue to express the same obligation). Do not edit `conventions.md`, `H3a-di-seam-skeleton.md`, or the cited spec pages — they already own the authoritative statements this fix points back to.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

The duplicated enforcement-posture prose creates a second normative surface for the ambient-scan / allow-list mechanism that `conventions.md` §*No globals* already owns; if the convention's ban set or allow-list model changes, the V8d/V8d-T bullets drift silently and an implementer reading them may treat the stale re-explanation as authoritative. Implementers can still produce a working leaf, so the defect is guidance-quality, not blocking.
