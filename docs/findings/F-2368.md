## Problem

`V9g` (and its test pair `V9g-T`) authors the `session_shutdown` handler's five-sub-step teardown, which directly consumes three cross-leaf contracts that neither leaf's `Deps` field names: the `V9m` drain-state API (`LoomRegistry.drain()` / `initDrainStateTag()` in sub-step 1), the `V9b` step-4 handler subscription that `V9g` back-fills, and the `V8d` `Clock` seam used by the sub-step-3 bounded await and sub-step-4 `Clock.clearTimeout`. `V9g`'s `Deps` is `V9g-T, V9a, V9e, V9h, V17a` and `V9g-T`'s is `V9e, V9h, V17a` ŌĆö each missing contract is reachable only transitively. The direct producerŌåÆconsumer relationship is therefore invisible at the leaf level and fragile against any later re-routing of the transitive edges the build order currently relies on.

**History:** All three undeclared edges trace to the initial plan build (c6a664e): `V9g`/`V9g-T` shipped with `Deps. V9g-T, V9e, V9h, V17a` / `V9e, V9h, V17a`, already carrying the five-sub-step `session_shutdown` handler, the `SHUTDOWN_AWAIT_CAP_MS` cap with its "fires at the cap" assertion, and reliance on the drain-state API ŌĆö none of `V9m` (then owned by `V9b`), `V9b`, or `V8d` declared as a direct edge. The later edit a6e2ecb added only the `V9a` edge to `V9g`'s Deps and touched neither `V9b`, `V8d`, nor the drain-state owner. The over-bundling fix (cb8737a) carved the `LoomRegistry` drain-state contract out of `V9b` into the new `V9m` leaf and ŌĆö per its own commit note ŌĆö explicitly preserved "the `V9h` `Deps` edge" while overlooking `V9g`'s parallel reliance, re-pointing the inception gap onto `V9m` without adding the corresponding `V9g` edge. The three missing direct edges have therefore been present since inception, with the `V9m` edge re-pointed by the cb8737a interaction.

## Solution approach

Add the missing direct `Deps` edges so each leaf names the cross-leaf contracts its teardown directly consumes. To `V9g`'s `Deps`, add `V9m` (sub-step-1 drain-state API), `V9b` (the step-4 handler subscription `V9g` back-fills), and `V8d` (the `Clock` seam used in sub-steps 3/4). To `V9g-T`'s `Deps`, add the edges to the contracts whose call-sites it asserts (the `V9m` drain calls and the `V8d` `Clock.clearTimeout` label).

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the contract-owning leaves `V9m`, `V9b`, and `V8d` themselves ŌĆö add the consuming edges to `V9g`/`V9g-T`'s `Deps` only; do not add reverse edges to or otherwise modify the owner leaves.
