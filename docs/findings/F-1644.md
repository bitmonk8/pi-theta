## Finding

The Slash-Command Argument Binding topic (`docs/spec_topics/binder.md` hub plus the four child pages it indexes) carries no consolidated *Non-goals* / *Deferred extensions* section. Two binder-area deferrals that are committed loom 1.0 seam-bearers are discoverable today only by reading the seam blockquotes in their respective child pages:

- The **binder refinement loop** (multi-turn `needs_info` negotiation, plus the user-visible rendering of `ambiguous.candidates` that the same future revision introduces) — pinned by the `> **loom 1.0 seam — binder refinement loop.**` blockquote at `docs/spec_topics/binder/binder-bypass-and-envelope.md` line 25, with its three loom 1.0 carriers BNDR-1/2/3 immediately below. The `ambiguous.candidates`-not-rendered rule that rides on the same deferral is restated in `binder/defaulting-system-note-echo.md` rule 5 and in the `ambiguous` row of `binder/determinism-cancellation-failure.md`.
- **Automatic context escalation** (a `needs_info` outcome triggering an automatic retry with `bind_context: session` attached) — pinned by the `> **loom 1.0 seam — automatic context escalation.**` blockquote at `docs/spec_topics/binder/binder-model-and-context.md` line 31.

The canonical inventory of both items lives in `docs/spec_topics/future-considerations/surface-extensions.md` (the *Binder refinement loop* bullet under *Surface extensions (loom 1.0 leaves a seam)*, and the *Automatic context escalation* bullet under *Surface extensions gated on an open design decision*), and each entry there back-links the binder-page seam carrier. The reverse direction — an implementer or auditor sitting on the binder topic and asking "what's deliberately *out* of binder scope for loom 1.0?" — has no single entry point: the binder hub (`docs/spec_topics/binder.md`) is a 12-line Contents stub, and the four child pages carry the seam blockquotes inline with their normative content. A reader has to know to scan all four for `loom 1.0 seam —` blockquotes, then cross-reference each into `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`, before reconstructing the loom 1.0 boundary.

A precedent for the consolidated index pattern already exists in the corpus: `docs/spec.md` carries an `### V1 non-goals` orientation aggregator that enumerates the seven cross-cutting non-goals and forward-links to `future-considerations/model-changes-and-non-goals.md` (the GOV-12 single-source page), and `docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/version-bump-intro.md` opens with a `### Non-goals` index of its procedure's exclusions. The binder topic, the conversation-drive topic, the audit subsystem topic, and the subagent topic — see *Related Findings* — are the topic pages that fail to follow the same pattern.
