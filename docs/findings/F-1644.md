## Problem

The Slash-Command Argument Binding topic (the `docs/spec_topics/binder.md` hub plus its four child pages) carries no consolidated non-goals / deferred-extensions section. Two committed loom 1.0 binder deferrals — the binder refinement loop (`binder/binder-bypass-and-envelope.md#v1-seam-binder-refinement-loop`) and automatic context escalation (`binder/binder-model-and-context.md#v1-seam-automatic-context-escalation`) — are discoverable only as inline seam blockquotes scattered across the child pages. A reader on the binder topic asking "what is deliberately out of binder scope for loom 1.0?" has no single entry point and must grep all four child pages for `loom 1.0 seam —` blockquotes and cross-reference each into `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** 83802db — spec: collapse to single typed argument style; add LLM-driven slash-command binder (2026-05-04, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The Slash-Command Argument Binding section was first authored in `83802db` (2026-05-04) as a section of the then-monolithic `spec.md`; no per-topic *Non-goals* subsection was included, even though the section already carried inline deferral notes ("A future revision may make this prompt user-overridable; V1 keeps it fixed for predictability"; "V1 has no `BinderError` variant in the `QueryError` union"). `0fa81fa` (2026-05-05) added the "structural distinction exists for the deferred refinement loop" rationale and `09c42c0` (2026-05-05) added the binder-invocation re-entrancy paragraph that became the automatic-context-escalation seam — both reinforced the deferral surfaces that motivate the missing aggregator. The corpus-wide non-goals consolidation in `0e12140` (2026-05-08, "Non-goals are not consolidated into a single section") established the `### V1 non-goals` aggregator on `spec.md` and the `## loom 1.0 non-goals` owner on `future-considerations/model-changes-and-non-goals.md`, but did not propagate the pattern down to topic-hub pages. `fecb504` (2026-05-04) split the spec into per-topic files and `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04) further sharded `binder.md` into four children, reducing the binder hub to a 12-line Contents stub — making the absence of a hub-level aggregator most visible — without introducing one. The defect is a single sustained omission across these authoring passes, never repaired; the binder topic has carried no consolidated non-goals section at any point in its history.

## Solution approach

Add an orientation-aggregator non-goals section to the `docs/spec_topics/binder.md` hub, ahead of the existing `## Contents` block. Have it forward-link the two seam carriers — `binder/binder-bypass-and-envelope.md#v1-seam-binder-refinement-loop` and `binder/binder-model-and-context.md#v1-seam-automatic-context-escalation` — and their canonical entries in `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`.

## Solution constraints

- The section is informative orientation only: it MUST NOT introduce new normative content or new obligation sites (which would trigger the GOV-22 REQ-ID coinage obligation); it forward-links the existing seam carriers rather than restating their rules.
