## Finding

The **Provider error mapping** opening paragraph in `docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/provider-error-mapping.md` closes its *Re-validation gate (loom 1.0.0)* sentence with: "*Wiring this re-validation into the bump procedure as a mechanical gate is a recognised post-loom 1.0.0 maintenance follow-up.*" That clause is a forward-looking process commitment about post-loom-1.0.0 roadmap work; it sits inside the same paragraph that authors loom 1.0.0's normative classifier contract (the `QueryError`-mapping table, the `MUST be re-validated on each upgrade` modal, and the routing of the manual re-run to item (i) of the editorial-review checklist).

Elsewhere the corpus uses a different shape for the same kind of deferral. `GOV-15` says "A conformance fixture suite … is a recognised post-loom 1.0.0 follow-up; see [Future Considerations](../future-considerations.md)" — the gap-statement lives on the obligation page, but the *roadmap* commitment is a one-line cross-reference into `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`, where the deferred work is inventoried (`Tooling deferrals (no loom 1.0 impact)` already lists the Pi-patch re-import cadence as a sibling deferral). The provider-error-mapping paragraph violates that convention: the post-loom-1.0.0 follow-up exists only as inline prose, no entry for it appears under `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`, and the trailing-sentence cross-reference from the sibling *Provider-owned-wording presupposition* paragraph ("See *Re-validation gate (loom 1.0.0)* above for the corresponding post-loom 1.0.0 maintenance follow-up") and from `version-bump-step2.md` item (i) ("the post-loom 1.0.0 maintenance follow-up noted under the *Re-validation gate* in **Provider error mapping** above") both anchor on this inline future-work sentence rather than on a Future Considerations entry.

The current obligation surface — the SHOULD-level fixture re-run pinned by item (i), the MUST-re-validated rule, and the version coupling to `@earendil-works/pi-ai` — is unaffected by the relocation; only the roadmap statement moves.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Remove the inline future-work clause from the *Re-validation gate (loom 1.0.0)* sentence and relocate it as a Future Considerations entry, mirroring the `GOV-15` ↔ `gov-15-fixture-suite` ↔ `future-considerations.md` pattern already established in the corpus. Concretely:

1. In `docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/provider-error-mapping.md`, replace the closing clause "*Wiring this re-validation into the bump procedure as a mechanical gate is a recognised post-loom 1.0.0 maintenance follow-up.*" with a one-line cross-reference of the shape "*CI-wired automation of this re-run is a recognised post-loom 1.0.0 follow-up; see [Future Considerations](../future-considerations.md#fc-provider-error-fixture-ci-gate).*" Keep the rest of the paragraph (the MUST-re-validated obligation, the SHOULD-run-the-fixtures routing through item (i), the version coupling to `@earendil-works/pi-ai`) unchanged.

2. In `docs/spec_topics/future-considerations/surface-extensions.md` under the existing `## Tooling deferrals (no loom 1.0 impact)` list (which already inventories the sibling "Automated cadence for re-importing Pi patches" deferral), add a new bullet pinned by a fresh anchor (e.g. `<a id="fc-provider-error-fixture-ci-gate"></a>`) describing the deferred CI-wired rerun of the provider-error overflow fixtures during the Pi version-bump procedure, with back-pointers to (a) the *Re-validation gate (loom 1.0.0)* paragraph and (b) item (i) of the editorial-review checklist in `version-bump-step2.md`.

3. Retarget the sibling *Provider-owned-wording presupposition* paragraph's trailing sentence ("See *Re-validation gate (loom 1.0.0)* above for the corresponding post-loom 1.0.0 maintenance follow-up") at the new Future Considerations anchor, or delete it entirely if the co-located sibling finding `Provider-owned-wording presupposition — dangling "See Re-validation gate above" cross-reference` is resolved in the same commit.

4. Retarget the `version-bump-step2.md` item (i) trailing sentence ("the post-loom 1.0.0 maintenance follow-up noted under the *Re-validation gate* in **Provider error mapping** above") at the new Future Considerations anchor as well.

The SHOULD-level item-(i) obligation, the MUST-re-validated rule, and the `npm test` fixture set are not edited.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

A reader scanning the normative paragraph for current loom 1.0.0 obligations also has to absorb a roadmap statement about deferred work, and the implicit convention demonstrated by `GOV-15` (gap-statement plus pointer to a Future Considerations entry) is silently broken — the deferred item is not catalogued anywhere a contributor browsing Future Considerations would find it. No implementer behaviour changes either way.
