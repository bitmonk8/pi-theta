## Problem

H7a's fourth Tests bullet (the "derived, not snapshotted" bullet) is not a run assertion: it describes how the committed golden transcript, golden diagnostics list, and permitted-code list are *sourced* from each Deps slice's already-established expected output, mandates that the composed turn ordering "is human-reviewed against `docs/spec.md` and the cited spec topics at first commit," states the permitted-code-list maintenance convention, and adds rationale clauses about the regression-target framing. None of this runs under `npm test`. Per `conventions.md` §Leaf format, a `Tests.` bullet states one assertion the leaf turns green at its exit gate, while `Adds.` is the descriptive home for what the leaf introduces and how its artefacts are constructed. Co-locating provenance description and a one-time manual review obligation with the run assertions misleads an implementer about H7a's automated coverage — the human review is either built as a check that cannot exist (a vacuous green) or silently skipped, leaving the goldens effectively snapshotted from an unreviewed run.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `c6e2a8b` (origin — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"golden transcript/diagnostics initial-correctness establishment\"", 2026-06-11); compounded by `052b019` ("…Real-host smoke pass criterion (e) names a permitted code set with no committed source", 2026-06-11) and `542395a` ("…H7a permitted-code-list Deps narrower than pipeline", 2026-06-12)
**History:** `git log -S` over `docs/plan_topics/H7a-integration-acceptance.md` shows the leaf was created at `e7e51cc` without this bullet. `c6e2a8b` added the entire "derived, not snapshotted … human-reviewed … at first commit" bullet to `Tests.` to resolve a prior review finding about golden initial-correctness establishment — i.e. the process-obligation-in-Tests defect was born with the bullet. `052b019` then folded the committed permitted-code list into the leaf and `542395a` broadened its per-Deps-slice provenance, both accreting further maintenance-convention prose into the same Tests bullet rather than into `Adds.`. The defect is the cumulative product of placing establishment/provenance text in `Tests.` across these three resolutions.

## Solution approach

Move the non-assertion content out of H7a's fourth Tests bullet into the leaf's `Adds.` field: the golden/permitted-code derivation provenance, the first-commit human-review obligation for the composed turn ordering, and the permitted-code-list maintenance convention. Delete the rationale-only clauses that restate the regression-target framing and explain why the ceiling-arbitration bullet needs no establishment note. Leave the runtime-run assertions — golden-transcript match, golden-diagnostics match, permitted-code-list-as-committed-superset, and the `CIO-5` ceiling-arbitration invariant — in `Tests.` so every remaining bullet is something `npm test` turns green.
