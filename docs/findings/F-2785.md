## Finding

`V9i` bundles the session-local subscribe-based completion detection (`PIC-42`) and the `agent_end` query-result extraction (`PIC-43`) — a separable completion-detection sub-surface (session-local `subscribe`, terminal-event selection, cancellation/transport short-circuits, trailing-text concatenation) — with the spawn/lifecycle/guard/abort core.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

Implementing all eight-plus obligations in one leaf produces an oversized single commit that no reviewer can hold in scope at once, degrading reviewability and inflating the diff surface. The leaf still ships a working subagent path, so pickability and spec-conformance are not blocked; the cost is structural — atomicity and the per-finding fix loop's convergence.
