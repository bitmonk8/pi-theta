## Problem

`runtime-event-channel.md` states an operator-facing observability obligation: every `loom-system-note` (including `display: false` notes) is converted by Pi's `convertToLlm` transformer into a durable `{ role: "user", content }` context entry on every subsequent provider call, with no opt-out at the loom 1.0 SDK pin, so parse errors, binder failures, panic notes, and always-log runtime events permanently consume context budget (feeding `ctx.getContextUsage()` and compaction). That obligation is interleaved with `pi.sendMessage` call-shape prose: its substance sits in the `custom-message-context-entry-presupposition` paragraph wedged between `**Delivery surface.**` and `**Empty `content` is legal.**`, plus a non-normative restatement in the `**Runtime event channel.**` block — both under headings that advertise SDK call mechanics rather than observability. An operator scanning the page for context-growth implications has no labelled subsection naming the concern. The content is present and correct; this is an organization defect, not a content gap.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-content-introduction
**Introducing commits:** `044985c` ("pi-loom spec: resolve \"loom-system-note re-enters model context as user-role text\"", 2026-05-07)
**History:** `git log -G 'convertToLlm'` shows the durable-context NFR first entered the corpus in `044985c`, added to the then-monolithic `spec_topics/pi-integration-contract.md` as the `Custom-message channel persistence and LLM-context entry` paragraph placed immediately after the `**Delivery surface.**` paragraph, plus the `display: false`-gating restatement in the `**Runtime event channel.**` block. The content was interleaved with the call-shape prose from its first appearance — it never had a dedicated observability subsection. `git log -S` on both the `Delivery surface` heading and the persistence paragraph confirms the later `78a2f94` ("Misplaced sections within the pi-integration-contract pages") only carried the whole block into the split `runtime-event-channel.md` page, preserving the same relative placement. The placement defect therefore dates to the content's inception, not to any subsequent edit.

## Solution approach

Restructure `runtime-event-channel.md` to gather the durable-context / no-opt-out / budget-growth material under a clearly-labelled operator/observability subsection, separated from the `pi.sendMessage` envelope and the per-variant `display`/`content` matrix. Keep the `convertToLlm` SDK-mechanism detail (transformer reference, `triggerTurn: false` not excluding context, the `excludeFromContext` analogy and re-audit hook) as the mechanism backing that subsection, leaving `**Delivery surface.**` and the variant prose carrying only call-shape text. Cross-reference the new subsection from the `display: false` gating rule in the `**Runtime event channel.**` block rather than restating the transform there.
