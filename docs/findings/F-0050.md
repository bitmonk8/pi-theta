## Finding

`H6a`'s **Release-gate acceptance (manual real-host smoke)** Tests bullet re-states the full enumeration of `H4a`'s live-host smoke pass/fail criteria inline — "no-error completion, binder output that validates against the binder envelope schema, observed cancellation propagation, the four fidelity-contract axes, and emitted `loom-system-note` codes within the committed permitted-code list." This enumeration is `H4a`'s authoritative criterion: `H4a` defines it as the labelled list (a)–(e) in its own Tests bullet, where (a) no-error completion, (b) binder-envelope validation, (c) observed cancellation propagation, (d) the four fidelity-contract axes, and (e) emitted codes ⊆ the committed permitted-code list.

The duplication occurs **twice** within the same `H6a` bullet: once where it characterises what "passed" means, and again in the trailing evidence-record sentence ("the observed result against `H4a`'s narrowed model-output-invariant criterion (…)"). Both copies restate `H4a`'s single-source criterion rather than pointing at it. `H4a` is the canonical owner of the live-host smoke and its pass/fail definition; `H6a` only confirms the run was executed and passed and records the evidence.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/H6a-live-corpus-activation.md`, in the **Release-gate acceptance (manual real-host smoke)** Tests bullet, strike both inline enumerations of `H4a`'s criteria and replace each with a bare cross-reference to `H4a`'s live-host smoke pass/fail criteria (a)–(e):

- First occurrence — the clause `where pass is the model-output-invariant criterion [H4a] defines for the live-host run — no-error completion, binder output that validates against the binder envelope schema (…), observed cancellation propagation, the four fidelity-contract axes, and emitted loom-system-note codes within the committed permitted-code list checked in alongside [H7a]'s fixture .loom — not exact reproduction of H7a's double-recorded goldens` becomes a reference of the form `where pass is [H4a]'s live-host smoke pass/fail criteria (a)–(e)`.
- Second occurrence — in the evidence-record sentence, the clause `the observed result against [H4a]'s narrowed model-output-invariant criterion (no-error completion, … committed permitted-code list …)` becomes `the observed result against [H4a]'s pass/fail criteria (a)–(e)`.

Leave the surrounding manual-gate framing, the Pi-SDK-pin / named-owner / run-date evidence-record obligation, and the commit-message recording requirement intact. `H4a` already labels its criteria (a)–(e), so the reference resolves; verify the labels are present in `H4a` before striking (they are, in `H4a`'s third Tests bullet) so the cross-reference does not dangle.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

`H4a` is the single source of the live-host smoke pass/fail criteria, yet `H6a` carries two verbatim copies. Any future edit to `H4a`'s (a)–(e) criteria silently leaves `H6a`'s copies stale, so a release-gate operator reconciling against `H6a` could apply an outdated pass/fail definition. No implementer is blocked; the risk is divergence of a duplicated normative criterion over time.
