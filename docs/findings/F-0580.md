## Finding

`V18d`'s **Adds.** field closes with a bolded `**Blast radius.**` sentence-group:

> **Blast radius.** A divergent pin's blast radius is every runtime leaf — it is real-host-only and is **not** witnessed by the double-backed acceptance gate above, which is why the pre-merge real-host smoke is the gate that closes the window. The static surface-inventory / `engines.node` / peer-dep / reason-snapshot gates are owned by [`V18c`](./V18c-version-bump-checklist.md).

`Blast radius` is not a leaf-template field. The leaf format (`leaf-template.md`, `conventions.md` §Leaf format) defines exactly five fields — **Spec.**, **Adds.**, **Tests.**, **Deps.**, **Ships when.** — and specifies `Adds.` as "one sentence — what the leaf introduces," descriptive and non-binding. The `**Blast radius.**` block is neither a description of what the leaf introduces nor a binding obligation; it is a risk characterisation (why a real-host smoke gate is needed) plus a scope restatement of `V18c`'s ownership.

The risk rationale belongs with the governance note that owns the smoke gate. `real-host-smoke-gate.md` already carries the full divergent-pin / real-host-smoke rationale (the double does not witness real-host divergence, so the manual pre-merge smoke is the closing gate). The `V18c`-ownership half is redundant with `V18d`'s existing **Deps.** edge on `V18c` and its **Tests.**/**Ships when.** references to `V18c`'s static build-time gates.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/V18d-version-bump-acceptance.md`, strike the bolded `**Blast radius.**` sentence-group from the **Adds.** field — both sentences, from `**Blast radius.**` through `... owned by [`V18c`](./V18c-version-bump-checklist.md).`. The remaining `Adds.` text (the runtime-evidence acceptance gate plus the pre-merge revert-path description) already states what the leaf introduces.

No relocation is required: the divergent-pin / real-host-smoke rationale is already carried by `real-host-smoke-gate.md`, which owns the smoke protocol, and the `V18c`-ownership statement is already pinned by `V18d`'s **Deps.** entry on `V18c` and by the `V18c` references in its **Tests.** and **Ships when.**. Leave `Tests.`, `Deps.`, and `Ships when.` unchanged.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

`Adds.` prose is non-binding by `conventions.md` §Leaf format, so the non-template `**Blast radius.**` pseudo-field does not mislead an implementer into wrong behaviour. It is organisational cruft: it introduces a sixth field-like label the leaf format does not sanction, duplicates rationale already owned by `real-host-smoke-gate.md`, and restates `V18c` ownership already pinned by `V18d`'s **Deps.**.
