## Finding

`V4e`'s **Adds** field closes two of its sentences with sub-clauses that
narrate the mechanism by which the leaf depends on `V16a` and `V5e`:
"… differs in kind from the four runtime first-enforcement sites —
**binding the seam via its `Deps` on `V16a`**" and "… not a synthetic
load-time signal, **binding `V5e` via its `Deps`**".

Both `V16a` and `V5e` are already named in `V4e`'s **Deps** field, and
that field is the authoritative declaration of the dependency edges. The
two sub-clauses restate that wiring as prose, adding no constraint the
**Deps** list does not already carry. They are meta-commentary about the
leaf's own structure rather than implementation direction for the V4e
author.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `e2b7e81` (2026-06-10) "pi-loom plan: resolve \"isolated cross-ceiling unit interface/authority undefined\""; `887c440` (2026-06-17) "pi-loom plan: resolve \"V4e declares no dependency on V5e depth-walk producer for ERR-16\""
**History:** `git log -S`/`-G` over `docs/plan_topics/V4e-pre-evaluation-failures.md` shows the two sub-clauses entered separately, each as a byproduct of a distinct earlier plan-fix commit. `e2b7e81` added the "binding the seam via its `Deps` on `V16a`" clause while wiring the V16a cross-ceiling edge; `887c440` added the "binding `V5e` via its `Deps`" clause while wiring the V5e depth-walk edge. Each fix correctly added a real Deps edge but also editorialised the Adds prose with the redundant "binding via its Deps" gloss; the cruft is accreted, not present since the leaf's creation (`c6a664e`).

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/V4e-pre-evaluation-failures.md`, in the **Adds**
field, strike both trailing dependency-narration sub-clauses:

- Remove "— … binding the seam via its `Deps` on `V16a`" so the first
  sentence ends at "… differs in kind from the four runtime
  first-enforcement sites." (drop the trailing " — binding the seam via
  its `Deps` on `V16a`").
- Remove ", binding `V5e` via its `Deps`" so the second sentence ends at
  "… (not a synthetic load-time signal)."

Leave the substantive content (the `consults`/`CIO-1` cross-route
description and the delegated live-carrier-witness statement for `V5e`)
intact; only the "binding … via its `Deps`" wiring restatements are
struck. The Deps field already declares both edges.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

The redundant sub-clauses add reading overhead to the Adds field and a
second, prose-form statement of dependency edges that can drift from the
Deps field if either is edited in isolation. No implementer diverges and
no gate is affected.
