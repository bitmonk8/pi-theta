## Finding

**Spec edits:** In `trust-boundary.md`, reword the grant-model clause to reflect the presupposition framing (access is bounded by whatever the pinned Pi minor's host process grants, which the runtime presupposes rather than audits) and cross-reference `overview-and-orientation.md` — Trust boundary and the version-bump per-extension privilege-scoping facet item (`version-bump-step2.md#bump-checklist-privilege-scoping-facet`). Do not assert an audited grant model.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory
